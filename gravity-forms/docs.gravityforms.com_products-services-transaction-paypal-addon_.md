# Products and Services Transaction Type in the PayPal Payments Standard Add-On

- [Transaction Type-specific Options](https://docs.gravityforms.com/products-services-transaction-paypal-addon/#h-transaction-type-specific-options)

**Note:** PayPal will deprecate Website Payments Standard (WPS) in January 2026, with full End Of Life in January 2027. PayPal Payments Standard Add-On will be affected, and merchants must migrate to PayPal Checkout to maintain payment functionality. Refer to the [PayPal Payments Standard Add-On End Of Life](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-admin/post.php?post=46377&action=edit) article for more information.

When using the PayPal Add-Ons for Gravity Forms, you can set various transaction types depending on your needs such as **Products and Services**, [Subscription](https://docs.gravityforms.com/products-services-transaction-paypal-addon/subscription-transaction-type-paypal-addon), and [Donations](https://docs.gravityforms.com/products-services-transaction-paypal-addon/donation-transaction-type-paypal-addon). The most common, **Products and Services**, is used to sell one-time purchases of physical or digital products.

## Transaction Type-specific Options

If you select **Products and Services** from the **Transaction Type** drop down, the option below will appear.

The **Payment Amount** field denotes which field will be used for the payment amount. It can be set to _Form Total_ or an additional field that you have configured.

[![PayPal Payments Pro Feed Settings Page Products](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/CleanShot-2021-07-09-at-12.12.29-1.png)](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/CleanShot-2021-07-09-at-12.12.29-1.png)

If you need more information on configuring feeds within the PayPal Payments Standard Add-On, review our article on [Creating a Feed for the PayPal Payments Standard Add-On](https://docs.gravityforms.com/create-feed-paypal-standard-add-on/).
