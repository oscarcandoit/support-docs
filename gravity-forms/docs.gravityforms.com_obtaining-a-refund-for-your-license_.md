The following is a summary only. For the full and current conditions of refunds, please review our official [refund policy](https://www.gravityforms.com/refund-policy/) terms.

## Which transactions are refundable?

We will refund **any** transaction you have made for a Gravity Forms subscription purchased through _gravityforms.com_ if your transaction date was **within the last 30 days**, no questions asked. This includes an original purchase or an upgrade transaction.

## Can I get a refund if I have upgraded my license?

Yes. The same conditions as stated above still apply.

If you have both purchased _and_ upgraded within thirty days, we will refund both transactions automatically. If your upgrade is within the last 30 days, only that transaction will be refunded.

Note that you **cannot** _downgrade_ an already upgraded license. So if you request a refund of an upgrade transaction, your license will still be immediately canceled (not downgraded to the previous license type) and any transaction older than 30 days for that license will not be refunded.

## How do I submit a refund request?

### Without access to a Gravity Forms Account

Simply [go to our refund page](https://www.gravityforms.com/refunds) and follow the instructions there.

### From your Gravity Forms Account

1. Log in to your [Gravity account](https://docs.gravityforms.com/updating-your-profile/) page. Go to **Licenses** and click **Manage Subscription**.
2. **If your subscription qualifies** for a refund, the Request Refund button will be presented below the license details on the left side of the screen.

[![Request Refund button in the Manage Subscription screen](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/image-48-848x1024.png)](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/image-48.png)

3. Click on **Request Refund** button to open a modal. Select a reason, then click¬†**Confirm Cancellation** **and** **Refund Request**.

[![Modal showing request refund confirmation promtp](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/image-76-1024x556.png)](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/image-76.png)

4. Back on the Manage screen, there is now an alert in blue.

[![Image showing Subscriptions screen after refund requested.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/image-77-1024x354.png)](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/image-77.png)

## What happens when I submit a refund request?

Your license and subscription will be canceled immediately at the time you submit your refund request. We will process the request within a few days, and you will receive a notification of completion. Note that your financial institution may take extra days to display your refund in your banking or credit card account.
