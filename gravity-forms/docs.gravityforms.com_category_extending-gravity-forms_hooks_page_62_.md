## Articles

[**gform\_post\_form\_duplicated** \\
This hook replaces the deprecated “gform\_after\_duplicate\_form” hook.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/gform_post_form_duplicated/ "gform_post_form_duplicated") [**gform\_post\_form\_restored** \\
This action is triggered after a form is restored from the trash, allowing further actions to be performed.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/gform_post_form_restored/ "gform_post_form_restored") [**gform\_post\_form\_trashed** \\
This action is triggered after a form is moved to the trash, allowing further actions to be performed.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/gform_post_form_trashed/ "gform_post_form_trashed") [**gform\_post\_form\_views\_deleted** \\
This action is triggered when a form view count has been reset to zero, allowing further actions to be performed.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/gform_post_form_views_deleted/ "gform_post_form_views_deleted") [**gform\_post\_multifile\_upload** \\
Allows further actions to be performed after multiple files have been uploaded.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/gform_post_multifile_upload/ "gform_post_multifile_upload") [**gform\_post\_note\_added** \\
This action is triggered after a note is added to an entry, allowing further actions to be performed.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/gform_post_note_added/ "gform_post_note_added") [**gform\_post\_notification\_save** \\
Fires after the notification is successfully saved.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/gform_post_notification_save/ "gform_post_notification_save") [**gform\_post\_paging** \\
Use this hook to perform actions after going to the next or previous page on a multi-page form.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/gform_post_paging/ "gform_post_paging") [**gform\_post\_payment\_action** \\
This action hook can be used to perform custom actions after processing of payment events.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/gform_post_payment_action/ "gform_post_payment_action") [**gform\_post\_payment\_callback** \\
