# Setting Up the Zoho CRM Add-On

- [Pre-Requisites](https://docs.gravityforms.com/setting-up-the-zoho-crm-add-on/#h-pre-requisites)
- [Zoho CRM Settings](https://docs.gravityforms.com/setting-up-the-zoho-crm-add-on/#h-zoho-crm-settings)

## Pre-Requisites

- [Download](https://docs.gravityforms.com/downloading-a-gravity-forms-add-on) and [install](https://docs.gravityforms.com/installing-a-gravity-forms-add-on/) the add-on
- A [Zoho CRM](https://www.zoho.com/crm/) account hosted by one of the following data centers:

  - accounts.zoho.com
  - accounts.zoho.com.au
  - accounts.zoho.eu
  - accounts.zoho.in
  - accounts.zoho.com.cn
  - accounts.zoho.jp
  - accounts.zohocloud.ca

**Note:** Zoho CRM’s API Version 1.0 has been deprecated and no longer works as of 12/31/2019. If you are using an older version of the Zoho CRM Add-on which uses an outdated version of the API, you will need to **update to a current version of the Zoho Add-on to properly authenticate to Zoho**

**Note:** Before connecting Gravity Forms to Zoho CRM, your user must be already added to an [organization](https://help.zoho.com/portal/en/kb/crm/organization-settings/personal-settings/articles/get-started-setup-organization-account).

## Zoho CRM Settings

01. Log into your WordPress admin dashboard.
02. On the left side of the navigation menu, hover over **Forms** and click on **Settings**.
03. From this page, click the **Zoho CRM** tab.
04. Click the **Click here to connect to Zoho CRM** button.

    ![Settings screen under Forms srcset=](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/Zoho-CRM-AddOn-Settings.png) Settings > Zoho for the Zoho CRM Addon for Gravity Forms”>
05. Wait while you are **Redirecting to Zoho CRM**

    ![Redirecting to Zoho CRM from the Zoho CRM Addon for Gravity Forms](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/Zoho-CRM-Add-On-Redirect-to-Zoho.png)
06. From the Zoho CRM login screen, **Sign in access to Accounts at Zoho**

    ![Sign into Zoho CRM from the Zoho CRM Add-on for Gravity Forms](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/Zoho-CRM-Add-On-Sign-into-Zoho.png)
07. Once you have signed into your Zoho Account, **Choose the services for Gravity Forms** from the list of CRM Organizations in your Zoho Account and click **Submit**.

    ![Choose Services to use with Gravity Forms from the Zoho CRM Addon for Gravity Forms](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/Zoho-CRM-Addon-Choose-Services.png)
08. Click the **Accept** button to allow the Gravity API access to the data in your Zoho account.

    ![Accept Authorization to allow Gravity API to access data in your Zoho account within the Zoho CRM Addon for Gravity Forms](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/Zoho-CRM-Addon-Accept-Data-Authorization.png)
09. You will be Redirected back to Gravity Forms from Zoho

    ![Redirecting back to Gravity Forms from Zoho within the Zoho CRM Addon for Gravity Forms](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/Zoho-CRM-Addon-Redirecting-back-to-GF.png)
10. You will now be connected to your Zoho Organization within Forms > Settings > Zoho

    ![Connected to Zoho Organization within Settings for the Zoho Addon for Gravity Forms](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/Zoho-CRM-Addon-Connected-Settings.png)
11. You can now [create Feeds for the Zoho Add-on](https://docs.gravityforms.com/creating-a-feed-for-the-zoho-crm-add-on/)
