# Form Actions

## Articles

[**gform\_post\_add\_feed** \\
The gform\_post\_add\_feed action will run after a feed is added to a form.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/gform_post_add_feed/ "gform_post_add_feed") [**gform\_post\_paging** \\
Use this hook to perform actions after going to the next or previous page on a multi-page form.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/gform_post_paging/ "gform_post_paging") [**gform\_preview\_body\_open** \\
The “gform\_preview\_body\_open” action is triggered when the body of the form preview page loads, allowing further actions to be done.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/gform_preview_body_open/ "gform_preview_body_open") [**gform\_preview\_footer** \\
This action is triggered when the footer of the form preview page loads, allowing further actions to be done.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/gform_preview_footer/ "gform_preview_footer") [**gform\_preview\_header** \\
The “gform\_preview\_header” action is triggered when the header of the form preview page loads, allowing further actions to be done.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/gform_preview_header/ "gform_preview_header") [**gform\_preview\_init** \\
The “gform\_preview\_init” action is triggered when the form preview page loads, allowing further actions to be done.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/gform_preview_init/ "gform_preview_init") [**gform\_update\_feed\_active** \\
The gform\_update\_feed\_active action fires immediately after a feed’s active status is updated.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/gform_update_feed_active/ "gform_update_feed_active") [**gform\_view** \\
This action allows additional actions to be performed based on the form view.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/gform_view/ "gform_view")
