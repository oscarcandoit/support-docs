# Using the Kit Creator Network

- [Screenshots](https://docs.gravityforms.com/using-the-kit-creator-network/#h-screenshots)

## **What is the Creator Network?**

The Creator Network is Kit’s feature to help you expose your audience to other creators in the Kit community. When someone submits your form, they will see your recommendations.

To learn more about the Creator Network, please review the Kit articles here: [https://help.kit.com/en/collections/3962696-creator-network](https://help.convertkit.com/en/collections/3962696-creator-network)

## Required Conditions

To use Creator Network Recommendations, the following conditions must be met:

- The form must contain an Email type field
- The Kit account is using a paid plan at [https://app.kit.com/account\_settings/billing/](https://app.convertkit.com/account_settings/billing/)
- The Kit account has a creator profile configured at [https://app.kit.com/creator\_profile/](https://app.convertkit.com/creator_profile/)
- The Kit account has Recommendations enabled at [https://app.kit.com/creator-network/](https://app.convertkit.com/creator-network/)
- **Ajax must be enabled** on the form embed method.

## How To Enable

This feature is enabled from within the Kit feed settings page. Go to **Form Settings → Kit → Form Settings**

[![Kit Form Settings](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/image-50.png)](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/image-50.png)
