## **What can I achieve with this feature?**

- **Leads Sync**: Sync leads automatically from Facebook Lead Ads across multiple pages with clear attribution to the source
- **Conversations**: Chat with customers across multiple Facebook pagesÂ using Facebook and Instagram chat integrations
- **Automation**: Build customised workflows to automate customer engagement across multiple Facebook pages
- **Reputation**: Manage reviews and your review responses across multiple Facebook pages

## **How do I connect multiple pages?**

**Step 1**: After enabling the feature in Labs, navigate to Settings > Integrations.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155035059804/original/e7FSaLLnj5xgfPe-kLFHCYP1HqO1yZpgNg.png?1729488858)

**Step 2**: Click on 'Connect', login to your Facebook account and approve all required permissions for LeadConnector to integrate with your Facebook account.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155035059933/original/PFZn4-GyKGBJVee4HhFBvtyC8LgeW06_sQ.png?1729489096)

**Step 3**: Select the pages that you wish to integrate.

You may choose to sync new leads only, or also sync previous 90 days' leads to start with.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155035059978/original/ObtRkrwAWQ4QIwBbenlp5s7nZYLBYDNfvA.png?1729489220)

**Step 4**: Map your Facebook Lead Ad form fields to Contacts' custom fields.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155035061463/original/9ikL0ynstvJ3pblYo6uqFXBU58cQtIbY1Q.png?1729491521)

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155035061456/original/Bld6_kC9DKsXWu4gjY-UEMIWNXw8tlsCWg.png?1729491507)![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155035061604/original/Bob8Y6YN09Zz5kOaOEOWIXQbvgWwzERZeg.png?1729491671)

## **Facebook Integrations - Manage pages**

You can manage the pages that wish you integrate by clicking on Manage pages and selecting/unselecting as per your preference. For example, if you don't wish to integrate one of the pages, you can unselect them in the Manage Pages menu.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155035061583/original/JJwmX6ofRx6Aa8-svzhs6h6YE0HxUM1cZA.png?1729491657)

## **Facebook Integrations - Settings**

You can manage the messaging capability across your integrated pages using the settings button. For example, if you don't wish to use one of the pages for messaging/chat, you can choose to disable the setting on that page.
