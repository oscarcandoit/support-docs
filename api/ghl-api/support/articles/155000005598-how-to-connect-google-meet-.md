## **What is Google Meet?**

Google Meet is a video conferencing tool that allows you to schedule and host virtual meetings. With **Google Meet integration**, you can automatically generate unique meeting links for each appointment scheduled through your calendar.

* * *

## **How to Connect Google Meet?**

Google Meet is automatically connected when you integrate your **Google Calendar**. There is no separate setup required for Google Meet.

### **Steps to Connect Google Meet:**

1. **Go to**: **Subaccount Settings** \> **Calendars** \> **Connections**
2. **Navigate to Video Conferencing >** Add New
3. **Click on** Google Meet and select **"Connect Google Calendar"**
4. **Authenticate** by selecting your Google account and granting necessary permissions
5. Once connected, **Google Meet will be successfully integrated.**

## **How to Remove Google Meet?**

Removing **Google Calendar** from your calendar connections will also remove Google Meet integration.

Similarly, disconnecting Google Meet will also remove **Google Calendar** from the integration.
