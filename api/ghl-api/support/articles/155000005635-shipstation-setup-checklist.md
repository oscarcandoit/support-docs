Follow these steps to ensure your ShipStation account is properly configured for integration.

* * *

## 1\. Add a ‘Ship From’ Location

1. **Log in** to your ShipStation account.

2. Go to **Settings** (gear icon) > **Shipping** \> **Ship From Locations**.

3. Click **Add a Ship From Location**.

4. Fill out the required details and save.

5. Make sure at least one location is listed as active.

## 2\. Enable ‘Package’ for All Active Carriers

1. In **Settings**, go to **Shipping** \> **Packages**.

2. Review the list of packages and ensure the **‘Package’** option is enabled for all active carriers you plan to use.

3. Save changes.


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155049058605/original/Jgej-Pkxkheyv3oQz2XCYfokJv3S7b1aZw.png?1751269742)

## 3\. Match Platform and ShipStation Account Currencies

1. Go to **Settings** \> **Account** \> **Your Account**.

2. Check your **Account Currency**.

3. Confirm that this currency matches your platform’s currency settings (the platform you’re integrating with ShipStation).

4. Update as needed to avoid mismatches during checkout and shipping calculations.


**3.1. Configuring in the platform:**

    Go to Settings > Business Profile > Business Physical Address > Select Country

_\\*\\* Based on the selected country - currency will be accordingly set_
