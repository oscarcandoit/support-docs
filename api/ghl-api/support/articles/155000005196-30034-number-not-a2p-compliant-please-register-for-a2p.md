## 30034: Number not A2P compliant. Please register for A2P  [Print](https://help.leadconnectorhq.com/support/solutions/articles/155000005196-30034-number-not-a2p-compliant-please-register-for-a2p\# "Print this Article")

Modified on: Mon, 4 Aug, 2025 at 4:45 AM

* * *

ERROR: 30034

* * *

**Description**

Messages sent to US numbers will not be delivered if they are sent from numbers that are not associated with an approved A2P 10DLC Campaign.

**Possible Causes**

- **You are sending messages to the US using a US 10DLC number that is not associated with an approved A2P 10DLC Campaign.**

**Possible Solutions**

- **Associate your US 10DLC number with a registered A2P Campaign by adding it to the corresponding Messaging Service via the leadconnector.**

- **Alternatively, you can also use a different number that is already associated with an approved A2P Campaign to send messages in the US.**
