## 21661: 'From' number is not SMS-capable  [Print](https://help.leadconnectorhq.com/support/solutions/articles/155000005091-21661-from-number-is-not-sms-capable\# "Print this Article")

* * *

**ERROR: 21661**

* * *

**Description**

Phone numbers must be SMS-capable. Visit the phone numbers page of your account to view a list of phone numbers that you own and to determine whether they are SMS-capable.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155045240572/original/oOtOWoEkOWqRQBqpDa5SEkFiDq3fjzRXMw.png?1744870299)

**Possible Causes**

The number you are using is not capable of sending messages.

**Possible Solutions**

- Check that you are using a Lead Connector phone number with SMS capabilities. You can see your purchased phone numbers and their capabilities in Settings > Phone Numbers > Manage Number > +Add Number.
- Purchase an SMS capable number from Settings > Phone Numbers > Manage Number > +Add Number.
