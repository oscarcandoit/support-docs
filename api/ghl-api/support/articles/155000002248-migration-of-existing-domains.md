### **Why is it good for you to migrate the domains**

- Funnels & websites are now 15% faster than before.
- Better caching of pages, easy prevention of DDoS attacks.
- More added security features.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155025272506/original/RywQnEDqmGFnoSwchrG47dq_LBZE2nwytg.png?1714457075)

### **How to switch?**

**Automated Flow:**

1. Go to Domains Page

2. Click on the **Migrate Domain** button.

3. The Domain Connect flow will initiate.

4. Simply follow the prompts, and update your DNS entries automatically.

5. That’s it! Your domain migration to CloudFlare is complete, without any fuss.![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155025272564/original/dJJI7oNtDA6lFgM9vXaUSIOAqkRGgpBbmA.png?1714457126)


**Manual Flow for Unsupported Providers:**

1. Click on the Migrate Domain button.

2. Update your DNS entries:
   - For CNAME records, point them to sites.ludicrious.cloud.
   - For the Apex record set the IP address to 162.159.140.166.
3. Click on the **Authorize domain** button verify that records.
4. Congratulations! Your domain is now on CloudFlare.
