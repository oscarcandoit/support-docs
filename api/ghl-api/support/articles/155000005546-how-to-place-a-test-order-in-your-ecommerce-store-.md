## How to Place a Test Order in Your Ecommerce Store?  [Print](https://help.leadconnectorhq.com/support/solutions/articles/155000005546-how-to-place-a-test-order-in-your-ecommerce-store-\# "Print this Article")

Modified on: Wed, 18 Jun, 2025 at 6:23 AM

* * *

Placing a test order is essential to verify that your checkout process, payment integration, shipping calculations, and order confirmation workflows are working as expected— **without charging a real payment method**.

This guide walks you through the steps to simulate a complete order cycle.

* * *

## ✅ When Should You Use a Test Order?

- Before launching your store to the public.

- After changing payment/shipping settings.

- To test the user experience from product selection to order confirmation.

- To confirm email/SMS notifications for customers and admins.


* * *

## ?️ Step-by-Step: Placing a Test Order

### **1\. Enable “Test Mode”**

> _![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155048466594/original/tiiprYrFxqv_jiqY5lHhgUmlxmL9SurGhA.png?1750245589)_

**Using Test Gateway**

- Go to **Sites > Stores > Select Store >** **Store Settings > Payment Mode**.

- Toggle **Test Mode** on.

- Save changes.


### **2\. Add Items to Cart**

- Navigate to your **live store frontend**. Preview you final Store.

- Add any product(s) to the cart.


### **3\. Proceed to Checkout**

- Fill out shipping and customer information as if you were a real customer.

- Ensure address fields, taxes, and shipping options appear correctly.


### **4\. Use Test Payment Details**

If you’ve enabled Test Mode, use one of the following **dummy card numbers** (if supported by your platform):

| Card Type | Number | Expiry | CVV |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Visa | `4242 4242 4242 4242` | Any future date | Any 3 digits |
| Mastercard | `5555 5555 5555 4444` | Any future date | Any 3 digits |

> ? _These numbers won't charge anything but will trigger the full checkout flow._

### **5\. Confirm Order**

- Complete the order and check that:

  - The order shows up in the **Payments >** **Orders**.

  - You receive the appropriate confirmation emails/SMS. Make sure you have enabled notifications in **Payments > Settings > Notifications**

  - The order status (e.g., pending, fulfilled) behaves as expected.

* * *

## ? Repeat with Different Scenarios (Optional)

To ensure full coverage, try test orders with:

- Different shipping methods or locations.

- Discount codes.

- Multiple product types (digital, physical, bundled).


* * *

## ? Clean Up (Optional)

After testing:

- Turn **Test Mode** off.

- Restore product prices if changed.


* * *

## ❓Troubleshooting Tips

- **Not receiving emails?** Check notification settings under **Payments > Settings > Notifications.**

- **Checkout page not loading?** Make sure your payment gateway is correctly set.
