## **ERROR: 30002**

* * *

**Description**

This message failed because your account was suspended after this message was queued but before it was sent by Lead Connector.

**Possible Causes**

1. Account was suspended for lack of funds after your message was queued but before it was sent by Lead Connector..
2. Account was suspended because Lead Connector detected a violation of our Acceptable Use Policy.

**Possible Solutions**

- Please contact support.
