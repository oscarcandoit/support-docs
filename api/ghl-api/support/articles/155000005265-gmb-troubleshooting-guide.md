## Ownership Conflict Issue:

**Error:** Resolve Ownership Conflicts - This location duplicates another location that is in good standing. If you have access to a location in good standing, use that location's id to perform operations. Otherwise, request access from the current owner.

Go to [https://business.google.com/add](https://business.google.com/add) and search for <<your business name>>.

[![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155046556543/original/j-dotp4OboNwfL3LX5XT6JcxUSkDlPxlHQ.jpeg?1747146130)](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155046556543/original/j-dotp4OboNwfL3LX5XT6JcxUSkDlPxlHQ.jpeg?1747146130)

If another user handles the business. It will come like below.

[![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155046556552/original/0aFhhiHtBtSlpbejqTEdNbHriy1OjfVi_Q.jpeg?1747146131)](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155046556552/original/0aFhhiHtBtSlpbejqTEdNbHriy1OjfVi_Q.jpeg?1747146131)

The profile is managed by under the email ID " [user-with-access@mail.id](mailto:user-with-access@mail.id)"

- If you know who the current profile owner is: Contact them directly and ask them to grant you the necessary access permissions. [Learn more about profile access types](https://support.google.com/business/answer/9178945?hl=en&ref_topic=4539640)

- If you don’t know the current owner: [Request access from the Business Profile owner](https://support.google.com/business/answer/4566671?hl=en).

- Go to [business.google.com/add](http://business.google.com/add).

  - Enter the business name "<<Your Business Name>>", then select it from the search results.
- Click Continue. You'll get a message that says someone else verified the profile. If you're authorized to manage the profile, you can request ownership from the current profile owner.

- Click Request Access and fill out the form.

- Click Submit. You receive an email notification and the current profile owner gets an email that asks them to contact you. Ensure you receive the confirmation email that lets you know your ownership request was successfully submitted.


Once the request has been raised, allow 3 days for a response. If you don’t get a response from the current owner within this time, click View request in the confirmation email. You can then claim and verify the profile in your account. [Learn more about ownership requests](https://support.google.com/business/answer/4566671?hl=en).

If you’re the profile owner: If you can’t sign in to your Business profile account with this email address, [learn how to get back into your account](https://support.google.com/accounts/troubleshooter/2402620).

## Comply with Guidelines:

The customer must mention the Google locationId(ListingID) in the form Additional Info.

#### BUSINESS\_LOCATION\_SUSPENDED:

**Error:** The business location is Suspended by Google to comply with guidelines.

The business location is suspended. To fix this issue, consult the [Help Center article](https://support.google.com/business/answer/4569145).

#### BUSINESS\_LOCATION\_DISABLED:

**Error:** The business location is Disabled by Google to comply with guidelines.

The business location is disabled. To fix this issue, consult the [Help Center article](https://support.google.com/business/answer/9334246).
