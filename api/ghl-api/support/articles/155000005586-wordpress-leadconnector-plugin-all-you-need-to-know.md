## **What is the LeadConnector Plugin?**

The LeadConnector WordPress plugin is a powerful conversion tool that empowers your website to capture, engage, and convert visitors with minimal effort. It seamlessly integrates high-converting widgets such as **chat widgets**, **forms**, **funnels**, **phone number pools, review widgets** and **calendars**— all designed to improve your lead acquisition strategy directly from your WordPress site.

Whether you’re looking to engage customers via SMS or email, display beautifully crafted funnels, or schedule appointments with ease, LeadConnector is your all-in-one toolkit for digital growth — without writing a single line of code.

## **Key Features & Capabilities**

### **Text-to-Chat Widget**

Embed a real-time chat widget directly on your site. This chat can be configured to automatically initiate conversations via SMS, guiding visitors from interest to conversion quickly and naturally.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155048811929/original/EO1G0ZY7mII9jWZp9pm8kVKnG31z_xPIWg.png?1750788276)

### **Funnel Page Embeds**

Bring high-converting funnel pages into your WordPress site with a simple shortcode or widget. No need for redirection or external tabs — visitors stay immersed on your site.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155048811832/original/PbqzfxWk3EWDrE7HH-owtA1fPURnbFn5gg.png?1750788126)

### **Form Integration**

Embed forms that directly capture user info—email, phone, name—and instantly sync them to your CRM or marketing workflows.

### **Email & Phone Tracking Pools**

Enhance attribution and follow-up through dynamic email and phone number tracking. Each form or funnel submission gets mapped to its source for better campaign performance measurement.

## **How to Get Started**

1. **Install** the LeadConnector Plugin from your WordPress dashboard or upload the .zip file.

2. **Activate** it and connect your account.

3. **Customize** which widgets you want: chat, calendar, form, funnel, etc.

4. **Paste Embed Links or Shortcodes** into any page or post.

5. **Launch** and start capturing leads instantly
