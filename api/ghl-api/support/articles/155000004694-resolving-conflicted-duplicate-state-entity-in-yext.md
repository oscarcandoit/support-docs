## Resolving Conflicted/Duplicate State Entity in Yext  [Print](https://help.leadconnectorhq.com/support/solutions/articles/155000004694-resolving-conflicted-duplicate-state-entity-in-yext\# "Print this Article")

If a business is already active on Yext through another provider, users may encounter a conflicted state. Follow these three steps to resolve the issue and regain access to Yext services.

### **1\. Information Gathering**

To proceed with cancellation, the business owner must collect the necessary details:

- **Entity ID**: Navigate to _Listings >> Business Entities >> Conflicted Entity (Entity ID)_.
- **Business Name**
- **Address**
- **Phone Number**



**![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155042666728/original/w7TVouN7Fe7dEJV18pAjWrQxhQ-0PKraqA.png?1741160148)**


This information is essential for Yext to verify and process the request.

### **2\. Contact Yext and Cancel the Service**

The **business owner must directly contact Yext** at **[locationconflict@yext.com](mailto:locationconflict@yext.com)** to request cancellation. Yext provides two cancellation options:

- **Cancel Immediately**
- **Cancel at the End of the Billing Cycle**

It is mandatory that business owner choose to cancel the Yext service immediately.

**Email Format:**

**To**: **[locationconflict@yext.com](mailto:locationconflict@yext.com)**

**Subject**: Listing stuck in conflicted state {Entity ID}

**Message Body:**

Hello Yext Team,

Our Online Listings request has moved to a conflicted state. Could you please assist us in resolving this?

**Business Details:**

- **Business Name:** \[Name of Business\]
- **Business Address:** \[Address of Business\]
- **Business Phone Number:** \[Phone Number\]

We appreciate your help and look forward to your response.

Best regards,

\[Your Name\]

\[Your Contact Information\]

\[Your Company Name\]

### **3\. Request Yext Service Again**

Once the cancellation is confirmed, the business can request Yext service again from the system.If the issue persists, please reach out to your administrator for further assistance.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155042666725/original/Vuhy5vZBufjjRQN-juAKmyTb2GSFTYHyYg.jpeg?1741160148)

Following these steps will ensure a smooth resolution of the conflict, allowing the business to restore its Yext services effectively.
