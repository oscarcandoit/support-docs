## **How to Buy a Toll-Free Number?**

**1\. Head into your Location/ subaccount where you would like to purchase a Toll Free number:**

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155001142321/original/kDqvmN_tnUQdOOguOd6Er68jB7Yz-SH9rg.png?1686843255)

**2\. Click on Settings at the bottom left:**

![Click on Settings](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155001141328/original/wvwlh5zpy6y0GkThYSWQg7VEJXn9muGe8A.png?1686842603)

**3\. Click on Phone Numbers**

![Click on Phone Numbers](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155001142461/original/c24mXf9P_sGxAa-gYVQYCVAjAYeHJp5i_Q.png?1686843337)

**4\. Click on Add Number**

![Click on Add Number](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155001141303/original/1kxMkHNZp8S8xPdGt-bgfNOrFujmXiUZbQ.png?1686842588)

**5\. Click on the Country dropdown:**

![Click on Canada](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155001141290/original/NFGAN28JeR0UdhKMas7LmQ91A7mbDPDHpw.png?1686842577)

**6\. Select United States in the dropdown**

![Click on United States](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155001141318/original/Sp9fkWpgL5YYCCK5uM3dLs9A1Oj42Js-AQ.png?1686842598)

**7\. Type the Area Code:**

Toll-free numbers are numbers that begin with one of the following three-digit codes: 800, 888, 877, 866, 855, 844 or 833. If it's not available for the one you are looking for, please reach out to support

![Type "833"](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155001141329/original/G39rjRVWMHENc1J3QwYiEBSjChAyjibIsw.png?1686842604)

**8\. Click on Search**

![Click on Search](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155001141330/original/VxbWkNp-CSw-XqmtJpNahlIbcuepPrGyYQ.png?1686842604)

**9\. Select any one of the phone numbers you like and hit save!**
