# **Why****Purchase a****Phone Number in LeadConnector?**

Purchasing a phone number in LeadConnector **allows you to send and receive calls and texts directly from the platform**. This gives users access to a wide range of local and toll-free numbers which is essential for automating outreach, managing client communications, and building a seamless experience across funnels, campaigns, and conversations.

```
Note: Phone numbers are managed at the sub-account level, not agency-wide. Each sub-account should have its own number for tracking and configuration purposes.

```

* * *

## **Key Benefits of Purchasing a Phone Number**

- Enables **two-way SMS and calling** inside LeadConnector.

- Establishes **local or toll-free presence** for branding and trust.

- Powers automated **workflows**, **call routing**, and **voicemail**.

- Integrates with **users**, **pipelines**, and **campaigns** for seamless communication.

- **Prevents the need to use personal phone numbers** for business tasks.

* * *

## **Steps to Purchase a Phone Number**

### **Step 1:** Navigate to Phone Number Settings

1. Click on **Settings** from your sub-account.

2. Select **Phone** **Numbers** under Business Services.

3. Click on the **\+ Add Number** button and select **Add Phone Number** from the drop-down.


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155045244718/original/cMSjyryIMI0OwkPh2o_PwN9nq_RFASzHHw.png?1744874995)

### **Step 2:** Selecting Country and Number

1. Select your **desired Country** from the list of available countries.

2. You can choose your **Number** from the available list of **Local** or **Toll Free** numbers.

3. Use the **Refresh Results** button to get a new list of available numbers.


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155045244715/original/5LzfnXSgvGB4larAQc7Wd7ZMsKz_GPNkvw.png?1744874993)

### **Search Filters**

You can use the filter option to search your desirable numbers on the basis of:

1. **Digit** or **Phrases** and match it with ( **first**, **last** or **any**) **part** of the number.

2. **Capabilities** (SMS, MMS, Voice)

3. **Number** **Type** (Local or Toll Free)


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155045244711/original/QxrCq8L6V4DTVZKetznnRqHrkzNK8KjM0A.png?1744874992)

### **Step 3:** Purchasing a Number

1. Select the **number of your choice**.

2. Click on **Proceed to Buy** button to complete the purchase.


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155045244713/original/NGALiSRgRqB0IsCFd0TA0w4Pc2KIbAIudw.png?1744874992)

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

**Q: Can I purchase multiple phone numbers for the same sub-account?**

Yes, you can purchase multiple numbers. This is helpful for managing different departments, services, or geographic regions within the same sub-account.

**Q: Do I need a separate number for each user?**

Not necessarily. One number can serve multiple users if routed correctly, but for personalized outbound messaging and tracking, dedicated numbers per user are recommended.

**Q: Will I be charged monthly for purchased phone numbers?**

Yes. you will be charged a small monthly rental fee for each number. Rates vary based on the type (local or toll-free) and region.
