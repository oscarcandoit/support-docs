## ? What is Competitor Analysis in Reputation Management?

Competitor Analysis is a powerful tool within the CRM's Reputation Management suite that allows agencies to compare their online reputation metrics with up to three competitors.

By automatically gathering and analyzing review data across platforms like Google, Facebook, Yelp, Foursquare, Trustpilot, and more, Competitor Analysis provides:

- **Reputation Score (0–100)** – Weighted rating based on reviews, volume, and responsiveness.

- **Sentiment Heatmap** – A visual matrix of positive, neutral, and negative reviews across competitors.

- **Keyword & Theme Analysis** – Trending positives and pain points, with clickable themes to view actual reviews.

- **Competitor Positioning Grid** – Interactive quadrant mapping strengths vs. weaknesses to reveal what customers love or dislike.

- **Ratings by Source** – Platform-specific insights (average rating, total reviews, response time, response rate).

## ⚙️ How To Setup Competitor Analysis

### Step 1: Navigate to Competitor Analysis

- From your dashboard, click **Reputation**.

- Select the **Competitor Analysis** tab.


### Step 2: Add Your Business

- Click **\+ Add Your Business**.

- Allow location permission and search for your business.

- Select the correct listing and click **\+ Add Your Business** again to confirm.


### Step 3: Add Competitors

- Click **\+ Add Competitor** (maximum of 3 competitors).

- Search for competitor businesses and select the correct ones.

- Click **\+ Add Competitor** to confirm.


### Step 4: Check Ratings by Source

- Scroll to **Ratings by Source** (Google, Facebook, Yelp, Trustpilot, etc.).

- For each platform, view:

  - **Average Rating** (e.g., 4.9 ⭐️)

  - **Total Reviews** (e.g., 295)

  - **Average Response Time** (e.g., N/A or 2 days)

  - **Response Rate** (% of reviews responded to)
