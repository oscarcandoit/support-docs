# How to Connect a HighLevel Sub-Account with Zapier

Modified on: Thu, 18 Sep, 2025 at 8:08 AM

Connect your **HighLevel sub-account** to **Zapier** (via the **LeadConnector** Zapier app) to automate lead capture, contact updates, and handoffs without code. This guide explains what the integration is, prerequisites, and how to connect/disconnect a sub-account.

# **What is Connecting a Sub-Account with Zapier?**

Zapier lets HighLevel exchange data with thousands of apps. Using the **LeadConnector** Zapier app, you authenticate with your **HighLevel CRM login**, then choose which **sub-account** to connect so Zaps can create/update contacts, move data, and trigger actions.

* * *

## **Key Benefits Connecting a Sub-Account with Zapier**

These advantages help you decide when to connect Zapier for process automation.

- **Fast automation**: Trigger actions across thousands of apps without custom code.

- **Sub-account scoped**: Choose the **specific sub-account** to keep data clean and separated.

- **Simple auth**: Use your HighLevel CRM login—no API keys required for this connection.

- **Easy maintenance**: Manage or disconnect the LeadConnector connection from Zapier’s App Connections page.


* * *

## **How To Connect a Sub-Account with Zapier**

Follow these steps in Zapier to connect the right sub-account via LeadConnector.

1. Log in to your **Zapier** account.

2. On the home screen, click **Create > Zaps**.



![](https://jumpshare.com/share/xDVyCpgR1s9xTAmLFqi9+/GIF+Recording+2025-09-18+at+5.36.42+PM.gif)

3. In the Zap editor, click on the **Trigger** block and select **LeadConnector** from the list of apps.



![](https://jumpshare.com/share/uPqO8dR3APk57YMPMLDP+/GIF+Recording+2025-09-18+at+5.40.48+PM.gif)

4. Select a **Trigger** **Event** like **Pipeline** **Stage** **Changed**.

5. Under the **Account** section of the trigger setup, click on the **Sign** **In** button.

6. You will be redirected to the **LeadConnector** **login** screen. Enter your **email and password** to proceed.



![](https://jumpshare.com/share/6whWjcEY4IgmCZSPx4Bt+/GIF+Recording+2025-09-18+at+5.48.01+PM.gif)

7. After logging in, you’ll be prompted to **select the sub-account** you want to connect with Zapier.





![](https://jumpshare.com/share/vtNYLQ3hqAOl7DyUJvIw+/zapier.png)

8. Once completed, your Zapier account will be successfully connected to the sub-account. You’ll see **LeadConnector** listed under “Accounts” in your Zap setup, confirming the integration is active.



![](https://jumpshare.com/share/hm2brQrt0wbFCxoJMCW7+/ZIQj0GLvuaBPyQ69KAvi4HSuCOKq-eUcJQ.png)

* * *

## **How to Disconnect Leadconnector from Zapier**

1. On the **Zapier home page**, click on **App Connections** in the left-hand menu.

2. Click the **three-dot menu (•••)** next to LeadConnector.

3. Select **Delete** to remove the connection.



![](https://jumpshare.com/share/rotqSlJ5S1zFQjUDpY7Q+/ZxwSlCb1GkvF_su1AZ8UqEs5LSkMsd4bCg.png)


* * *

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

**Q: Is the Zapier app named “HighLevel”?** In Zapier, use **LeadConnector**—it’s the official, whitelabeled HighLevel connection.

**Q: Can I change the connected sub-account later?**

Yes—add a new **LeadConnector** account connection in Zapier and select the other sub-account, then switch the account used in your Zaps.

**Q: How do I fully disconnect?**

In Zapier → **Apps → App Connections**, locate **LeadConnector**, open the **•••** menu, and **Delete** the connection.

**Q: Do I need API keys for this?**

Not for the standard LeadConnector connection flow; you sign in with your CRM credentials.
