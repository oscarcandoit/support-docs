# One Question at a Time (Survey Builder)

Modified on: Thu, 13 Nov, 2025 at 3:28 PM

**“One Question at a Time”** is a setting in the Survey Builder that shows one question per screen when someone is filling out your form. It creates a simpler, more focused experience for your audience.

* * *

**TABLE OF CONTENTS**

- [What It Does](https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/155000005354-one-question-at-a-time-survey-builder-#What-It-Does)
- [How to Turn It On](https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/155000005354-one-question-at-a-time-survey-builder-#How-to-Turn-It-On)
- [Things to Know](https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/155000005354-one-question-at-a-time-survey-builder-#Things-to-Know)

* * *

## **What It Does**

When enabled:

- Each survey question appears on a separate slide both for your visitors and in the builder preview.

- This layout helps keep people engaged by showing only one thing at a time.


* * *

## **How to Turn It On**

1. Open your survey in the builder.

2. Click the **Styles & Options** button in the upper right-hand corner



![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155058342726/original/sG5UNkuxN1srBdNlOCPq4RG-Qbaz-3AZcg.png?1763069126)

3. Under **Survey Type**, select the **O** **ne Question at a Time** option. Once enabled, you’ll see each field on its own slide while editing just like how it appears to users.



![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155058342832/original/QA8-46pgaMDiBsmvlDdl6--gbvnm5DRXHQ.png?1763069219)


* * *

## **Things to Know**

- If you’re using conditional logic, turning this on may affect how logic works. You’ll see a warning before anything changes.





![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155058342855/original/X1WLkPs3z1_xo-wGKTofr4FE-kQsug4hWg.png?1763069250)

- This setting only changes how the survey is displayed it doesn’t remove or modify your questions.
