# Contacts – Revamped List View & SmartLists

Modified on: Tue, 14 Oct, 2025 at 10:11 AM

# **What is the Revamped Contacts List View & SmartLists?**

HighLevel’s updated Contacts module brings together an all-new List View and SmartLists 2.0 into one seamless experience. Designed with clarity and efficiency in mind, this release equips you with robust tools like drag-and-drop column management, a powerful filter builder with AND/OR logic, and enhanced sharing controls—all aimed at making contact organization simpler and more action-oriented.

* * *

## **Key Benefits of the new List View & SmartLists**

These improvements are crafted to boost productivity and enhance your workflow by providing:

- A modern, intuitive interface that aligns with the rest of the CRM pages

- Quick column management with search and drag-and-drop functionality for effortless organization

- Advanced filtering using AND/OR logic to build complex queries effortlessly

- Streamlined SmartLists featuring integrated sorting, field management, and refined sharing & permissions

- Reduced setup time, empowering you to focus more on nurturing customer relationships

* * *

## **Contact List View**

The reimagined Contact List View offers a cleaner and more consistent display, making it easier to scan and manage your contacts.

## **SmartList 2.0**

SmartList 2.0 revolutionizes how you create and manage contact groups by combining multiple features into one cohesive workflow. Benefit from:

- An advanced filter builder that applies both AND/OR logic for detailed queries

- Easy field management to add, remove, or reorder contact information

- Integrated sorting that lets you quickly organize your contacts by various criteria

- Enhanced sharing and permissions settings to control who can view or edit your SmartLists

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

**Q: How do I know I’m using the new Contact List View?**

A: When you navigate to Contacts, the updated, modernized view is applied automatically.

**Q: What makes SmartList 2.0 different from the previous version?**

A: SmartList 2.0 integrates advanced filtering, sorting, field management, and refined sharing options into a single workflow for enhanced efficiency.

**Q: How can I provide feedback on these new features?**

A: Feedback can be shared directly with your support representative or via the designated feedback channels provided within HighLevel.

**Q: Are the new sharing and permissions settings more secure?**

A: Yes, the enhanced sharing options grant more granular control over who can view or edit SmartLists, ensuring better data privacy and security.

**Q: Can I create complex contact queries with the new filter builder?**

A: Absolutely! The filter builder’s AND/OR logic allows you to craft detailed queries that precisely match your criteria.
