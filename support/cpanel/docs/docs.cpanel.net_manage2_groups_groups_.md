## Groups

Last modified: _2021 December 23_

* * *

## Overview

This Groups interface allows you to add, edit, delete, and search for groups.
The interface defaults to showing 100 groups at a time. Use the menu below to change the number of groups shown.

## List of groups

This interface lists your groups and displays the following parameters for each group:

- _ID_ — The group’s ID number.
- _Group_ — The group’s name.





Note:




If this group is the _Main Group_, _Main_ appears next to the _Group Name_.


- _Licenses_ — How many licenses are associated with a group.
- _Address_ — First line of the group’s address.
- _City_ — Group’s city.
- _Postal Code_ — Group’s postal code.
- _Region_ — Group’s state or province.
- _Country_ — Group’s country.
- _Created_ — Timestamp of the date group was created.

## Add a group

To add a group, perform the following steps:

1. Click the _Add Group_ text box.
2. Enter a name and address for the group.
3. At the bottom of the interface, select the _Set as Main Group_ checkbox to make this group the main license group.





Note:




This step may not be available to all companies.


4. Click _Save_ to save your new group. Click _Close_ to discard your group.

## Export CSV

To export CSV, click _Export CSV_ in the navigation bar. This downloads a file.

## Toggle Columns

To change parameters that create the list of groups, click _Toggle Columns_, and select or deselect the desired parameters.

## Search Groups

To search for a group, enter the group’s name in the text box on the right side of the interface.

## Managing groups and licenses

### View Licenses

To view all group licenses, click the arrow in an entry’s row and click View Licenses.

### Transfer Licenses

To transfer licenses between groups, perform the following steps:

1. Click the arrow in a group’s row and click _View Licenses_.






Note:




Your account must contain at least two groups to allow a license transfer.


2. Select the group from the menu.
3. Click _Transfer_ to save changes. Click _Close_ to discard changes.

### Edit a group

To edit a group, perform the following steps:

1. Click the arrow in a group’s row and click _Edit Group_.
2. In the _Name_ text box, enter the new license group name.
3. Edit any of the address boxes as needed.
4. Select or deselect the _Set as Main Group_ option.
5. Click _Save_ to save your changes. Click _Close_ to discard your changes.

### Deleting a group

To delete a group, perform the following steps:

1. At the end of each row, there is an icon to the right of the _Created_ column. Click the arrow.
2. Click _Delete Group_.
3. Choose another license group to transfer the licenses associated with the group you wish to delete.





Note:




Your account must contain at least two groups to allow a license transfer.


4. Click _Transfer_ to save your changes and delete the group. Click _Close_ to discard your changes.
