## View Contracts and Agreements

Last modified: _2022 November 30_

* * *

## Overview

The _View Contracts and Agreements_ interface allows you to download contracts and agreements associated with your account.

## View and download contracts

[One company](https://docs.cpanel.net/manage2/company/view-contracts-and-agreements/#one-company1764175699065576408)

If you only have one company available, the interface automatically displays the PDF files.

Click the desired PDF to download that file.

[Two or more companies](https://docs.cpanel.net/manage2/company/view-contracts-and-agreements/#two-or-more-companies1764175699065651005)

To download a contract or agreement, perform the following steps:

1. Use the menu to select your company.

2. Click _Go_. The contracts and agreements will appear below as PDF files.

3. Click the desired PDF to download that file.
