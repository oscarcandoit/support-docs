## cPanel Dovecot Solr

Last modified: _2025 September 18_

* * *

### 9.8.0-5

* * *

##### **2025-09-18**

- CPANEL-49306: Fix post install script on RHEL based systems

### 9.8.0-4

* * *

##### **2025-09-16**

- Fix CPANEL-48037: Ensure that the solr config uses jre-11 and not jre-1.8x

### 9.8.0-2

* * *

##### **2025-07-02**

- Build for AlmaLinux 10 and Ubuntu 24
- HB-8189: Update to 9.8.0
- Fixes CVE-2024-52012 and CVE-2025-24814

### 8.11.4-1

* * *

##### **2024-11-04**

- HB-8016: Update to new upstream 8.11.4
- Fixes CVE-2024-45216 and CVE-2024-45217

### 8.11.3-1

* * *

##### **2024-02-14**

- BOO-3805: Update to new upstream 8.11.3

- Fixes CVE-2023-50291, CVE-2023-50292, CVE-2023-50298, CVE-2023-50386

- HB-7259: Fix disable of service on upgrade for Ubuntu

- Remove vestigial logic and init scripts relating to CentOS 6 and lower

- Ensure firewall script with ‘stop’ arg is executed in ExecStopPost

- CPANEL-43492: Fixed startup for Ubuntu installs when previously installed

- CPANEL-43495: Fixed queuing the startup of the cpanel-dovecot-solr service


### 8.11.2-8

* * *

##### **2023-11-29**

- CPANEL-43528: Also require java-1.8.0-openjdk-headless for CentOS 6 and 7

### 8.11.2-7

* * *

##### **2023-11-02**

- CPANEL-43451: Tweak requires block to fix unresolvable update on Alma 8
