## Social Media Management (Powered by Social Bee) Change Log

Last modified: _2025 October 20_

* * *

### 1.7.0-1

* * *

##### **2025-10-20**

- BEE-1258 - Roll-out first page variant for all users.
- BEE-1196 - New signup flow creates duplicates if site scan fails + allow flow to continue on crawl failure.
- BEE-1312 - Prevent advancing with unselected domain in cPanel extension.

### 1.6.0-1

* * *

##### **2025-06-02**

- DUCKS-2938: Various user experience improvements in terms of landing page, loaders and generated posts layout

### 1.5.0-1

* * *

##### **2025-04-24**

- DUCKS-2347: Enable Social Media Management to additional select partners for cPanel

### 1.4.0-1

* * *

##### **2025-04-08**

- DUCKS-2517: Made improvements to the AI integration to enhance responsiveness and reliability.

### 1.3.0-1

* * *

##### **2025-03-10**

- DUCKS-1824: Updates include:
  - Add tooling to reinitialize the feature on configuration changes, such as server license updates, that affect feature access.
  - Prevent users with bookmarked internal links to the Social Media
    Management page from accessing the page when it is disabled.

### 1.1.0-1

* * *

##### **2025-02-25**

- DUCKS-1771: Enable Social Media Management feature by default for cPanel
versions 126 and above.

### 1.0.5-2

* * *

##### **2025-02-17**

- DUCKS-2040: Release Social Media Management plugin (Powered by SocialBee) to SafeList companies

### 1.0.4-1

* * *

##### **2025-02-12**

- DUCKS-2136: Install the plugin on Nexeon Technologies, Inc. servers.

### 1.0.3-1

* * *

##### **2025-02-11**

- DUCKS-1713: Public Release of the Social Media Management plugin (Powered by SocialBee) for
cPanel Direct & Siteocity.
- Feature Showcase for SocialBee

### 1.0.1-11

* * *

##### **2025-02-03**

- DUCKS-1974: Initial release of the Social Media Management (Powered by SocialBee) for select company IDs
