## Overview

This feature allows you to transfer multiple accounts to a reseller.

## Transfer multiple accounts

To transfer multiple accounts, perform the following steps:

1. Select the new reseller who will own the accounts you select.
2. Click _Submit_.
3. Select the accounts to transfer to the desired reseller.
4. Click _Change Owner of Selected Accounts_.
