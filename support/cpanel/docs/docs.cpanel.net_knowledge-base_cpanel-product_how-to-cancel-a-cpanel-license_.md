## How to Cancel a cPanel License

Last modified: _2025 May 29_

* * *

## Overview

This document describes how to cancel a cPanel license in the [cPanel Store](https://store.cpanel.net/).

## Cancellation timeline

Important:

You can cancel a licence within 72 hours of purchase for a full refund. After 72 or more hours, license purchases are **not** eligible for a refund.

You must cancel your license and [submit a request](https://support.cpanel.net/requests/new) to cPanel Customer Service within 72 hours of purchase to request a refund.

When you cancel a cPanel license within 72 hours of initial purchase, the following applies:

- Cancellation occurs immediately.
- The license is eligible for a refund. For more information, read the [Refund eligibility](https://docs.cpanel.net/knowledge-base/cpanel-product/how-to-cancel-a-cpanel-license/#refund-eligibility) section below.

When you cancel a cPanel license 72 or more hours **after** purchase, the following applies:

- The license remains active until the end of the month.
- The license is **not** eligible for a refund.

## How to cancel a cPanel license

Note:

Before cancelling a cPanel license, you **must** log in to your [cPanel Store](https://store.cpanel.net/) account.

To cancel a cPanel license in the cPanel Store, perform the following steps:

1. Select _View My Licenses_ in the _Orders/Licenses_ menu.
2. Locate the desired active license in the table.
3. Under _Status_, click _Active_ next to the license that you wish to cancel. A new interface will appear.
4. Click _Request Cancellation_. The _Cancel License_ interface will appear.
5. Select the license that you wish to cancel.
6. Answer the survey questions and then click _Submit Cancellation Request_.
7. The system will submit your cancellation reequest.

Important:

When you cancel a license, the associated IP address cannot use prorated pricing for 30 days. For more information about this limitation, send an email to [cPanel Customer Service](mailto:cs@cpanel.net).

Note:

For more information about renewing a canceled license, read our [How to renew a canceled or expired license](https://docs.cpanel.net/knowledge-base/cpanel-product/how-to-renew-a-canceled-or-expired-cpanel-license/) documentation.

## Refund eligibility

New licenses are eligible for a full refund within 72 hours of purchase. Licenses canceled after that time are **not** eligible for a refund.

To request a refund, [submit a request](https://support.cpanel.net/requests/new) to cPanel Customer Service within 72 hours of purchase.

Refunds usually take 5 to 10 business days to process.
