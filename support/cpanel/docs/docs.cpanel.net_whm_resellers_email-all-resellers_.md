## Email All Resellers

![](https://docs.cpanel.net/img/whm-icons/email_all_resellers.svg)

_Valid for versions 82 through the latest version_

#### Version:

#### [82](https://docs.cpanel.net/whm/resellers/email-all-resellers/)

Last modified: _2024 June 13_

* * *

Looking for this interface?

Note:

Your [hosting provider](https://docs.cpanel.net/knowledge-base/cpanel-product/how-to-identify-your-hosting-provider/) can enable or disable this interface for resellers in WHM's [_Edit Reseller Nameservers and Privileges_](https://docs.cpanel.net/whm/resellers/edit-reseller-nameservers-and-privileges) interface ( _WHM >> Home >> Resellers >> Edit Reseller Nameservers and Privileges_).

## Overview

This interface allows you to email every cPanel reseller simultaneously.

Note:

To send a message to the cPanel users on your server, use WHM’s [_Email All Users_](https://docs.cpanel.net/whm/account-functions/email-all-users/) interface ( _WHM » Home » Account Functions » Email All Users_).

## Email resellers

To email your resellers, perform the following steps:

1. Enter the name, email address, subject, and body of the message that you wish to send.
2. Click _Send_.
