## Overview

This interface allows you to delete a locale.

Note:

You **cannot** remove the locales that come with cPanel & WHM.


## Delete a locale

To delete a locale, perform the following steps:

1. Select a locale from the _Locale_ menu.
2. Click _Delete_.
3. Click _Yes_ to confirm that you wish to delete the locale.

Note:

To update the system’s list of locales, click _rebuild the locale databases_ at the bottom of the interface.
