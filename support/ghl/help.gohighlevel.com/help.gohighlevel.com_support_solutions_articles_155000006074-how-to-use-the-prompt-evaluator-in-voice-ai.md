# How to Use the Prompt Evaluator in Voice AI

Modified on: Mon, 22 Sep, 2025 at 7:59 AM

HighLevel’s Voice AI Prompt Evaluator gives instant feedback on your voice prompts, offers clear fixes, and lets you re-check changes so conversations improve fast. This article shows how and when to use the tool to speed up prompt work.

## **What is Voice AI Prompt Evaluator?**

The Voice AI Prompt Evaluator is a feature within HighLevel’s Voice AI module. It allows you to analyze and refine your voice agent prompts instantly by delivering clear improvement suggestions and feedback. With the ability to mark suggestions as resolved or waived and re-evaluate prompt changes, this tool streamlines prompt optimization to ensure your interactions are engaging and effective.

* * *

## **Key Benefits of Voice AI Prompt Evaluator**

Unlock a range of advantages that help you enhance your Voice AI prompts:

- **Instant clarity:** Real-time checks with specific, actionable guidance.

- **Your call:** Mark items resolved or waive what doesn’t apply—no clutter.

- **Proof of progress:** One-click re-evaluation to validate each edit.

- **Faster cycles:** Faster, iterative workflow that shortens prompt cycles.

- **Better calls:** Stronger prompts lead to clearer, more natural interactions.

* * *

## **How To Setup Voice AI Prompt Evaluator**

Getting started with the Voice AI Prompt Evaluator is simple and straightforward. Follow these steps to begin leveraging this powerful tool within HighLevel’s Voice AI module:

01. Log in to your sub-account.

02. In the left navigation, click **AI Agents**.

03. Click the **Voice AI** tab to open the Voice AI workspace.



    ![](https://jumpshare.com/share/NqepKuKGSuUxViYk1lHQ+/Screen+Shot+2025-08-21+at+8.42.16+PM.png)

04. Decide whether to **edit an existing agent** or **create a new agent**.

05. On the row for your agent, click the **three dots** next to the **Last updated** field.

06. Click **Edit** to open the agent editor.



    ![](https://jumpshare.com/share/ciMcJ5Uz0AUv4XmH5r8X+/Screen+Shot+2025-08-21+at+8.44.34+PM.png)

07. In the agent editor, open the **Agent Goals** section.

08. If the agent shows **Basic mode**, click **Switch to Advanced mode**.



    ![](https://jumpshare.com/share/LYcnv3PMj0duiNBHbC2E+/Screen+Shot+2025-08-21+at+8.46.40+PM.png)

09. In the Prompt area, click **Evaluate**.



    ![](https://jumpshare.com/share/Q5jOzGT87FUHogTwirJe+/Screen+Shot+2025-08-21+at+8.48.00+PM.png)

10. An **Evaluation results** panel appears on the **right-hand side** with suggested changes and findings.

11. Click any item in **Evaluation results** to expand its details and recommendation.




    - Click **Resolve** on the selected evaluation item to accept and apply the recommendation.

    - Click the **Waive** button to **disregard** the suggested change.
12. Toggle **Show critical only** to **on** to display only high-priority items.

13. You can **Evaluate** **as many times as you want** after resolving or disregarding items to refine the prompt and improve results.



    ![](https://jumpshare.com/share/xhylONu6Q8rR95nXDCQS+/GIF+Recording+2025-08-21+at+8.49.41+PM.gif)

## **Structured Feedback Loop**

The structured feedback loop encourages agile experimentation with your prompts. Rapidly test different approaches, implement updates, and view the results immediately. This iterative process enables you to steadily optimize your voice agent interactions.

- Iterate prompt development quickly

- Experiment with multiple prompt styles

- Accelerate production of high-quality voice interactions

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

**Q: How does the Voice AI Prompt Evaluator work?**

It analyzes your prompt in real time, offering clear, actionable suggestions to help you optimize your Voice AI interactions.

**Q: Can I edit my prompt after an evaluation?**

Yes, you can modify your prompt directly and immediately re-run the evaluation to verify improvements.

**Q: What does it mean to waive a suggestion?**

Waiving a suggestion means that you choose to disregard a piece of feedback if it isn’t applicable to your specific scenario.

**Q: Are additional permissions required to use this feature?**

No additional permissions are necessary; the feature is readily available within the Voice AI module in HighLevel.

**Q: Can I run multiple evaluations on the same prompt?**

Absolutely! The design of the tool encourages iterative testing and refinement, letting you re-evaluate your prompt as many times as needed.
