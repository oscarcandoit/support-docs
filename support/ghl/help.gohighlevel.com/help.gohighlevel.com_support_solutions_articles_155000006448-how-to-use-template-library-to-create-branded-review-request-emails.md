# How to Use Template Library to Create Branded Review Request Emails

Modified on: Wed, 5 Nov, 2025 at 8:50 AM

Discover how the Template Library Integration in Email Review Requests empowers you to craft personalized review emails effortlessly. This guide explains how to choose between a fully customizable blank canvas and a time-saving collection of pre-built templates, streamlining your Reputation Management process in HighLevel.

# **What is Template Library Integration ?**

The Template Library Integration in Email Review Requests is a feature that simplifies your email creation process by offering two distinct approaches. You can either design emails entirely from scratch, enabling total creative freedom, or select from a curated Template Library that provides pre-designed layouts.

This dual-mode capability ensures that whether you aim to express a completely custom vision or prefer a quicker setup, you have the ideal option to maintain your brand’s distinct voice while efficiently requesting reviews.

* * *

## **Key Benefits of Template Library Integration in Email Review Requests**

This feature is designed to boost productivity and consistency, letting you deploy professional review request emails with ease. The key benefits include:

- **Create from Scratch:** Enjoy full control over every design element with a blank email canvas.


- **Template Library:** Gain immediate access to a collection of polished, ready-to-use templates that you can quickly tailor to match your brand identity.


- **Faster Campaigns:** Reduce the time spent on email design by leveraging pre-built layouts, so you can focus on engaging with your clients and improving your online reputation.


* * *

## **Create from Scratch**

This option provides a completely blank canvas, allowing you to design every aspect of your review request email with precision. With complete creative control, you can add custom text, images, and styling elements that truly echo your brand’s personality.

- Customize layouts, colors, and fonts to ensure alignment with your brand guidelines.


- Enjoy the flexibility to experiment and build unique campaigns from the ground up.

## **Template Library**

The Template Library option offers a collection of professionally designed email templates that serve as a fast-start alternative for your campaigns. Each template is crafted to be easily customized, ensuring that even pre-built designs can be fine-tuned to reflect your brand’s look and feel.

- Access a variety of layouts tailored for review request emails.


- Adjust pre-designed templates with your content and branding elements effortlessly.


- Save significant time in campaign creation while maintaining a consistent brand presentation.

## **How To Setup Template Library Integration in Email Review Requests**

Proper setup guarantees that you can efficiently leverage this feature to meet your business needs. Follow these straightforward steps to get started:

### **Accessing Email Requests**

Navigate to the **Reputation** section from the main menu. Select **Settings** from the Reputation dropdown options. Click on **Email Requests**.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155054822493/original/R1LPi-bjtVrfyRyA3id_dfKCoVq1xzScdA.png?1759150030)

### **Turn On Email Requests**

Here, you’ll see a toggle switch—just turn it **ON** to activate email requests. You can also decide:

- When the first email should go out (e.g., 1 hour after check-in).

- How often to resend until the contact clicks.

- The maximum number of retries.


This ensures your review requests are consistent without overwhelming your audience.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155054825605/original/XecLmNL3FYhlv25UJidNe4Ym7-89RE6enQ.png?1759151200)

### **Start a New Email**

Click the **Create New** button. You’ll be given two options:

- **Create New Template** – start with a completely blank design.

- **Import from Template Library** – browse pre-built layouts and pick one that fits your style.


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155054826432/original/svodne8ma1FErhR-yontNkvakinQr3iR9w.png?1759151468)

### **Build a Template from Scratch**

If you choose the blank canvas(Create new Template option), you’ll use the drag-and-drop builder.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155054826758/original/UyNwpP58oPp9YrCcDwchpYldpkg5DO4kPQ.jpeg?1759151606)

### **Email Builder**

Here you can add:

- **Text, images, and buttons** to structure your message.

- A **Review Link** so contacts can leave feedback easily.

- **Logos, social icons, and dividers** to make your email look polished.


When you’re happy with the design, click **Save**.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155054827467/original/7TwmeGsMsxWFbkNsIWN6IQG2-0gG037wgg.png?1759151812)

### **Use the Template Library**

If you prefer a faster route, select **Template Library**. You’ll see a collection of professionally designed templates, organized by categories such as **Business Coaching, Financial, Creative**, and more.

Choose a design you like, swap in your text and branding, and you’re ready to go.

### ![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155054826515/original/ZS9wMI1CUSPX_IZ8Bkn8ZLT6w1AN18B1sg.jpeg?1759151529)

### **Manage Your Templates**

Once your templates are saved, you’ll find them listed in the Email Requests dashboard. Next to each template, click the **three dots (⋮)** to open management options:

- **Preview & Edit Details** – See how your email looks before sending, and adjust key details like subject line or sender name.

- **Edit Template:**Open the full drag-and-drop editor to make design changes.

- **Clone:**Duplicate an existing template if you want to create a variation without starting from scratch.

- **Restore:**Bring back a previously deleted template if you change your mind.

- **Delete:**Permanently remove a template you no longer need.

This makes it easy to keep your library organized and fine-tuned.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155054823856/original/aemVab2LroeAv506fHfw7UJ3FYe1HZYLgA.png?1759150462)

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

**Q: What does the Template Library provide in this feature?**

It gives you immediate access to a selection of professionally designed templates that can be further customized to fit your brand.

**Q: Where can I access this feature?**

This tool is available in your HighLevel account under Reputation → Settings → Email Requests → Create Email.

**Q: Can I still design a custom email if I choose a template form?**

Yes, once you select a template, you retain the ability to modify text, images, colors, and layout elements to perfectly align with your brand.

**Q: Is the "Create from Scratch" mode available alongside the Template Library?**

Absolutely. You can choose between a blank canvas for total creative control or a pre-built template for faster setup.

**Q: Do I need any technical expertise to use this feature?**

No technical skills are necessary; the interface is designed for ease of use across all skill levels in HighLevel.

**Q: Who benefits most from this integration?**

Both agencies and clients can streamline their campaign creation process, saving time while ensuring consistency in review request emails.
