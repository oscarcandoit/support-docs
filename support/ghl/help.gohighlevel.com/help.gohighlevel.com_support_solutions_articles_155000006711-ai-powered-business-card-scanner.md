# AI Powered Business Card Scanner

Modified on: Wed, 15 Oct, 2025 at 11:44 AM

## **Overview**

Introducing **AI Business Card Scanner** — the fastest and smartest way to capture new contacts on the go. Designed for professionals who network at events, conferences, or client meetings, this new **AI-powered feature** lets you scan physical business cards directly from your mobile app and automatically extract key details like name, phone number, email, and company with industry-leading accuracy.

No more lost cards or manual data entry. The system instantly organizes contact information, allows you to **add tags** for segmentation, and saves your new contact to your CRM — all in seconds.

* * *

## **Please Note**

- The AI Business Card Scanner is currently available on the **mobile app only**.

- Each sub-account includes **50 free AI scans/refinements per day**.

- Beyond the free tier, additional scans are charged at **$0.02 per scan**.

- You must grant **camera access** to use this feature.

## **What is the AI Business Card Scanner?**

The **AI Business Card Scanner** uses advanced machine learning to instantly extract and populate business card details into your CRM contact fields.

It recognizes key details like:

- Name

- Phone number

- Email address

- Company name

- Designation and website (if available)


Each scan achieves over **98% accuracy**, ensuring clean and reliable data capture. You can also scan **both sides** of the card to capture every piece of information and refine the scan further with a single tap.

* * *

## **Why It Matters**

For anyone who networks frequently — at conferences, client meetings, or trade events — collecting and organizing business cards can be tedious and error-prone.

This feature eliminates that friction entirely:

- **Instant digitization:** Turn every card into a CRM contact in seconds.

- **AI accuracy:** Extracts and refines data with 98%+ accuracy.

- **Seamless organization:** Add tags instantly to trigger automations or categorize contacts.

- **Reduced manual effort:** No typing, no lost leads, no backlog of unentered data.


**Result:** Every handshake becomes a saved lead — organized, actionable, and ready for follow-up.

* * *

## **How to Scan a Business Card from the Mobile App**

Follow these simple steps to use the AI Business Card Scanner:

01. **Open the Mobile App.**

02. Go to **Contacts** or use **Quick Actions → Add Contact**.

03. Tap **Scan Business Card**.

04. **Capture the front side** of the card using your phone camera.

05. (Optional) **Scan the back side** for additional details.

06. **Add Tags:** On the creation screen, add or select tags to group contacts for automations and campaigns.

07. Review the auto-filled contact details (name, phone, email, company, etc.).

08. (Optional)

    - Toggle **Save to Media Storage** to keep the scanned card image.

    - Toggle **Add to Files & Folders** to attach the image to the contact record.
09. If you notice any inaccuracies, tap **Refine with AI** — the system will reprocess and enhance the data with near-perfect precision.

10. Tap **Save Contact**, and you’re done!


Your new contact is instantly added to the CRM, fully categorized and ready for automation or follow-up.

## **FAQs**

**Q: Can I scan both sides of a business card?**

Yes. You can capture both front and back sides to ensure no information is missed.

**Q: What happens if the extracted details are inaccurate?**

Tap **Refine with AI** to instantly enhance extraction accuracy using a secondary AI pass.

**Q: Where are the scanned images stored?**

You can optionally save them to **Media Storage** or attach them directly under **Docs & Files** for each contact.

**Q: Is this feature available on desktop?**

Currently, the AI Business Card Scanner is available on the **mobile app only**.

**Q: How are scans counted in the free tier?**

Each scan or AI refinement counts as one use toward your daily limit of 50 per sub-account.
