## **Access from HighLevel University**

Here’s a clear, step-by-step guide to access the course

1. From **Agency View**, click **University** in the left sidebar.

2. Select **Go to HighLevel University**. This will open the **HighLevel University group** in a new browser tab.

3. In the top navigation menu of the HighLevel University group, click **Learning**.

4. Locate the course **The Course Creator’s Playbook** and click **Open** to begin.
