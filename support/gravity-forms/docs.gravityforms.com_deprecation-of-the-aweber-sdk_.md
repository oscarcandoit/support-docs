# Deprecation of the AWeber SDK

- [Deprecation Details](https://docs.gravityforms.com/deprecation-of-the-aweber-sdk/#h-deprecation-details)

## Deprecation Details

The Gravity Forms AWeber Add-On’s AWeber SDK will be removed starting with version 4.2.0. If you had an active connection before version 4.0.0, be sure to reconnect your AWeber account and update your integration settings **before** upgrading.

If you don’t do this ahead of time, your AWeber connection might stop working, which could affect how contacts are added to your lists.

Refer to [this](https://docs.gravityforms.com/setting-up-aweber/) article for more information about setting up the Gravity Forms AWeber Add-On.
