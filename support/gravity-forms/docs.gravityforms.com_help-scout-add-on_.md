# Setting Up the Help Scout Add-On

- [Custom Help Scout App](https://docs.gravityforms.com/help-scout-add-on/#h-custom-help-scout-app)
- [Add-On Framework Hooks](https://docs.gravityforms.com/help-scout-add-on/#h-add-on-framework-hooks)

## Pre-Requisites

- [Download](https://docs.gravityforms.com/downloading-a-gravity-forms-add-on) and [install](https://docs.gravityforms.com/installing-a-gravity-forms-add-on/) the add-on
- A [Help Scout](https://www.helpscout.com/) account with a [role of Account Owner, Administrator, or User](https://docs.helpscout.com/article/15-user-roles-and-permissions). Help Scout does not allow users assigned the Light User role API access.

## Setup

### Standard

1. Log into your WordPress admin dashboard.
2. On the left side navigation menu, hover over **Forms** and click on **Settings**.
3. From this page, click the **Help Scout** tab.
4. Click the **Click here to authenticate with Help Scout.** button to connect to your Help Scout account. If you wish to use a custom Help Scout app, see the instructions [below](https://docs.gravityforms.com/help-scout-add-on/#custom-help-scout-app).

[![Help Scout Connect Settings](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/image-6.png)](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/image-6.png)

5. Click the **Authorize** button on the Help Scout page to authorize the connection.

Once you have successfully authenticated your account, you will see a green checkmark next to **Authenticated with Help Scout**.

[![Help Scout Custom App Authorize](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Help-Scout-Custom-App-Authorize.png)](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Help-Scout-Custom-App-Authorize.png)

6. Now you’re ready to begin [creating a feed for the Help Scout Add-On](https://docs.gravityforms.com/creating-a-feed-for-the-help-scout-add-on/).

### Custom Help Scout App

If you wish to use a custom Help Scout app, follow these instructions:

1. Log into your WordPress admin dashboard.
2. On the left side navigation menu, hover over **Forms** and click on **Settings**.
3. From this page, click the **Help Scout** tab.
4. Click **I want to use a custom HelpScout app.**. The page will reload with new OAuth and App fields.

[![](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/CleanShot-2021-06-11-at-15.13.17.png)](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/CleanShot-2021-06-11-at-15.13.17.png)

5. The OAuth Redirect URI field will be filled in automatically.
6. Enter in your **App Key** and **App Secret**. These are found in your Help Scout account under My Apps.

[![Help Scout Custom Connect Settings](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/CleanShot-2021-06-11-at-15.34.31-1024x558.png)](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/CleanShot-2021-06-11-at-15.34.31.png)

7. Click the **Click here to authenticate with Help Scout.** button to connect to your Help Scout account.
8. Click the **Authorize** button on the Help Scout page to authorize the connection.

[![Help Scout Custom App Authorize](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Help-Scout-Custom-App-Authorize-300x246.png)](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Help-Scout-Custom-App-Authorize.png)
9. Once you have successfully authenticated your account, you will see a green checkmark next to **Authenticated with Help Scout**.



Now you’re ready to begin [creating a feed for the Help Scout Add-On](https://docs.gravityforms.com/creating-a-feed-for-the-help-scout-add-on/).
