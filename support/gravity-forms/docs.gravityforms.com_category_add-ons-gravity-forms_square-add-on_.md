# Square Add-On

## Articles

[**{square\_receipt\_url} Merge Tag** \\
The {square\_receipt\_url} merge tag outputs the URL to the receipt hosted by Square for the transaction associated with the current entry.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/square_receipt_url-merge-tag/ "{square_receipt_url} Merge Tag") [**Setting Up the Square Add-On** \\
Setup your Square Add-On so you can connect and authenticate to either your Live or Sandbox Square environment. These steps are necessary to manage your feeds and process your form submissions.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/setting-up-square-add-on/ "Setting Up the Square Add-On") [**Creating a Square Sandbox Account for Testing** \\
Details how to to sign up for a Developer Account with Square and configure a Sandbox Test Account to use with the Square Add-on in Sandbox Mode.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/creating-square-sandbox-account-for-testing/ "Creating a Square Sandbox Account for Testing") [**Using the Square Add-On** \\
The official Gravity Forms Square Add-On allows you to quickly and easily capture credit card payments with WordPress and your Square account. This article gives a quick introduction to the Add-On, and links to all the associated user guides.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/using-the-square-add-on/ "Using the Square Add-On") [**Setting up a Square Compatible Form** \\
Describes the steps for creating a form that will be compatible for payment processing using the Square Add-On.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/setting-up-square-compatible-form/ "Setting up a Square Compatible Form") [**Creating a Feed for the Square Add-On** \\
Details the process for creating a Feed for the Square Add-on to connect and transfer payment information to your Square account on Form submission.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/creating-feed-for-square-add-on/ "Creating a Feed for the Square Add-On") [**Managing Square Subscriptions in Gravity Forms** \\
This article describes the features for managing Square “Subscription” transaction types, available in the official Gravity Forms Square Add-On.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/managing-subscriptions-in-the-square-add-on/ "Managing Square Subscriptions in Gravity Forms") [**“Authorize Only” Transactions in the Square Add-On**\\
“Authorize Only” is a Feed Setting option within the official Gravity Forms Square Add-On that allows you to validate payment information only on submission, and then capture the payment at a later date.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/authorize-only-transactions-square/ "“Authorize Only” Transactions in the Square Add-On") [**Issuing Refunds with the Square Add-On** \\
This article describes how to initiate a refund for a previously received payment within the official Gravity Forms Square Add-On.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/refunds-square/ "Issuing Refunds with the Square Add-On") [**Square Changelog** \\
View the Change Log for the Square Add-on.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/square-change-log/ "Square Changelog")
