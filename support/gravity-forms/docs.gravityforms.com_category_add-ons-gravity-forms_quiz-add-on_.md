# Quiz Add-On

## Articles

[**{quiz\_passfail} Merge Tag** \\
Displays either Passed or Failed based on the Pass/Fail settings configured in the Quiz Settings.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/quiz_passfail-merge-tag/ "{quiz_passfail} Merge Tag") [**{quiz\_score} Merge Tag** \\
Displays the total score for the quiz or for a specific quiz question. The score is affected by weighted scoring.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/quiz_score-merge-tag/ "{quiz_score} Merge Tag") [**{quiz} Merge Tag** \\
Displays the specific quiz question along with your answer and the correct answer.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/quiz-merge-tag/ "{quiz} Merge Tag") [**Setting up a Quiz Field** \\
Learn how to setup the required Quiz fields so you can create your Quiz form with the Quiz Add-On.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/setup-the-quiz-add-on/ "Setting up a Quiz Field") [**Configure Quiz Settings** \\
This article shows you how to configure your grading settings for the Quiz Add-On.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/configure-quiz-settings/ "Configure Quiz Settings") [**Using the Quiz Add-On** \\
The Gravity Forms Quiz Add-On allows you to quickly create quizzes that are automatically graded or scored when the form is submitted. Learn how to install, setup, and use the Quiz Add-On.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/using-quiz-add-on/ "Using the Quiz Add-On") [**Viewing Quiz Results** \\
Learn how to view the results and statistics from your Gravity Forms quiz.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/viewing-quiz-results/ "Viewing Quiz Results") [**Quiz Scoring** \\
This article describes, using examples, how weighted scoring is calculated within the official Gravity Forms Quiz Add-On.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/quiz-scoring/ "Quiz Scoring") [**Quiz Merge Tags** \\
The Quiz Add-On introduces several new Gravity Forms Merge Tags that can be used anywhere within Gravity Forms where merge tags are supported. This article details each of them.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/quiz-merge-tags/ "Quiz Merge Tags") [**Quiz Add-On Changelog** \\
View the change log for the Quiz Add-On.](https://docs.gravityforms.com/quiz-change-log/ "Quiz Add-On Changelog")
