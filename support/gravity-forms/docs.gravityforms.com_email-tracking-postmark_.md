# Email Tracking with Postmark

By using the Postmark Add-On to send form notifications, you have the ability to take advantage of additional email features within Postmark. These features include the ability to track data within the form notifications, such as if the notification has been opened. In this article, we’ll show you how to enable tracking within your form notifications.

1. Access the form notification that is configured to use Postmark. If you don’t already have a notification that is using Postmark, see our article on [sending form notifications with Postmark](https://docs.gravityforms.com/form-notifications-postmark/).
2. Scroll down in the settings until you see an option labeled **Email Tracking**.
3. Check the box labeled **Enable open tracking for this notification** to enable tracking of who has opened your form notifications.
4. Save your settings.

[![PostMark Email Tracking](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/CleanShot-2021-07-09-at-13.18.30.png)](https://docs.gravityforms.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/CleanShot-2021-07-09-at-13.18.30.png)

That’s all there is to it! With tracking settings enabled, you will be able to better track if a form notification has been opened.
