WP Fusion extends [Convert Pro](https://www.convertpro.net/) to allow you to target call-to-actions based on a logged-in user‚Äôs tags in your CRM.

#### [\#](https://wpfusion.com/documentation/lead-generation/convert-pro/\#setup) Setup

After designing your CTA, navigate to Configure¬†¬ª Target, and expand the WP Fusion section.

![](https://wpfusion.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/convert-pro-2-1024x633.jpg)

Once you‚Äôve turned on the targeting rule you‚Äôll be able to select a tag in your CRM from the dropdown.

- The default logic is ‚Äú _Show only to users who have this tag‚Äù._ With this selected, the CTA will only be displayed when a user is logged in and has the selected tag.
- If you set the logic to ‚Äú _Hide from users who have the tag‚Äù_ the CTA will be shown only to guests and registered users who do¬†_not_ have the specified tag.
