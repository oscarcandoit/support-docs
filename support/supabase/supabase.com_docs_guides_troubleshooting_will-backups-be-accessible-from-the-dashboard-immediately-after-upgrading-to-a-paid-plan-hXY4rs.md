# Will backups be accessible from the dashboard immediately after upgrading to a paid plan?

Last edited: 2/21/2025

* * *

We are currently taking up to 7 daily backups that will be available for you once you upgrade. Keep in mind that we might no longer make daily backups for free projects in the future.

But at the moment, you'll be able to access them after upgrading.
