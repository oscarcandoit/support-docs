# Team How-Tos

Validated on 19 Jun 2018 • Last edited on 17 Apr 2025

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/teams.svg)

How to Create New Teams

Use the DigitalOcean Control Panel to create new teams and share resources. Group related teams into an organization for consolidated billing, payment, and invoicing.

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/teams.svg)

How to Manage Team Membership

Invite new members to a team, resend or revoke team invitations, change roles, or remove existing members from a team.

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/teams.svg)

How to Use Custom Roles

Create a custom role with only the permissions you choose for more granular control over team access to resources.

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/teams.svg)

How to Require Secure Sign In for Teams

Increase team security by requiring all team members to sign in with Google, GitHub, or a DigitalOcean account with 2FA.

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/teams.svg)

How to Manage SSH Public Keys on DigitalOcean Teams

Add public SSH keys to a DigitalOcean team to be able to automatically configure SSH key authentication during Droplets creation.

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/teams.svg)

How to Manage SSL Certificates on DigitalOcean Teams

Manage your team’s SSL certificates for use with DigitalOcean Load Balancer SSL termination and custom Spaces CDM endpoints.

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/teams.svg)

How to View Security History

View a team’s security history to see a record of actions taken on the team, like creating or deleting resources and API tokens.

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/teams.svg)

How To Participate in DigitalOcean's Referral Program

If you enjoy DigitalOcean and want to share it with friends and colleagues, you can earn credit by referring others with a team’s unique referral link.

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/teams.svg)

How to Delete Teams

Delete a team to remove it from the control panel.

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/teams.svg)

How to Configure Single Sign-On for Teams

Enable single sign-on (SSO) to let team members sign in to DigitalOcean with a single set of credentials from an OIDC-compatible identity provider (IdP), like Okta.
