# Z-Stream

Generated on 10 Apr 2024
from [the Z-Stream catalog page](https://marketplace.digitalocean.com/add-ons/z-stream)

Z-Stream is a fully-featured product development platform designed to help teams manage their projects from start to finish - from planning to launch. Z-Stream uniquely meets the demands of the modern agile team, offering a wide set of features to foster intuitive organization and the successful creation and development of products. Z-Stream supports roadmapping, testing, agile boards, Gantt Chart visualizations, integration with Git version control software, automatic report generation and more. It has been built on Node and Angular, and offers easy integration with G Suite. Keep all your data in one place, empower every team member, and track progress in real time. Increase productivity with Z-Stream’s user-friendly design and intuitive interface. Create professional, client-oriented reports in seconds and manage your team from anywhere.
