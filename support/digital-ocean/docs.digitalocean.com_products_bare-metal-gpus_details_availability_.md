# DigitalOcean Gradient™ AI Bare Metal GPUs Availability

Validated on 13 Nov 2024 • Last edited on 14 Nov 2025

DigitalOcean Gradient™ AI Bare Metal GPUs are dedicated, single-tenant servers with 8 GPUs of various models that can operate standalone or in multi-node clusters.

Gradient AI Bare Metal GPUs are available in the AMS and NYC regions.

Learn more on the [regional availability page](https://docs.digitalocean.com/platform/regional-availability/).
