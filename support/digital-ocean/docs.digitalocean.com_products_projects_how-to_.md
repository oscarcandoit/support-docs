# Projects How-Tos

Generated on 25 Nov 2025

Projects let you organize your DigitalOcean resources into groups that fit the way you work. Create projects that align with the applications, environments, and clients that you host on DigitalOcean.

## Getting Started

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/projects.svg)

How to Create Projects

Create projects to organize your resources in ways that align with how you use them.

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/projects.svg)

How to Move Resources between Projects

Move resources between projects, either one at a time or in bulk, to reorganize as your infrastructure changes and grows.

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/projects.svg)

How to Delete Projects

Delete an empty, non-default project to remove it from your account.
