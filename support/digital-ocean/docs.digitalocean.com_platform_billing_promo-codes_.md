# Promo Codes

Validated on 18 Mar 2022 • Last edited on 13 May 2025

You can redeem a promo code to apply credits to your team.

Your team must have a [valid payment method](https://docs.digitalocean.com/platform/billing/manage-payment-methods/) in order to redeem a promo code and create Droplets or other resources.

## Redeem a Promo Code

To redeem a promo code, go to your team’s [**Billing** page](https://cloud.digitalocean.com/account/billing). In the **Overview** tab, find the **Promo code** section.

![The Promo Code section of the Billing page](https://docs.digitalocean.com/screenshots/billing/promo-codes.542fa95f762eab660ffd184b5e51cd33ce8124e1104dd7090b0ee6e8a85734a5.png)

Enter the case-sensitive code in the promo code field and click **Apply Code**. Once you successfully redeem a promo code, the credits are automatically applied to your team and the **Promo code** section of the **Billing** page no longer appears.

You can [view your team’s credits](https://docs.digitalocean.com/platform/billing/invoices/#credits) in the control panel and on your team’s invoices.

## Promo Code Limits

The following limits apply to promo codes:

- **Each team can redeem one promo code**. If you redeemed a promo code in the past, you cannot redeem another. Your promo code history is visible in the **Billing history** section.

- **Promo codes are non-transferable**. You cannot transfer promo code credits to other teams.

- **Promo codes expire after 1 year**. Unlike PayPal pre-payments, which do not expire, credits from promo codes expire one year after you redeem them.

- **Promo codes do not apply to [Marketplace](https://marketplace.digitalocean.com/) Add-Ons**.
