# Custom Images How-Tos

Generated on 25 Nov 2025

Custom images are Linux and Unix-like images you import to DigitalOcean. You can create Droplets based custom images, which lets you migrate and scale your workloads without spending time recreating your environment from scratch.

## Getting Started

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/custom-images.svg)

How to Upload Custom Images

Upload custom images to a DigitalOcean account to create Droplets based on them.

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/custom-images.svg)

How to Create Droplets from Custom Images

Create Droplets based on custom images you’ve uploaded to your account.

## Custom Image Management

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/custom-images.svg)

How to Add Custom Images to Additional Regions

Make your custom image available in additional datacenter regions.

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/custom-images.svg)

How to Delete Custom Images

Delete a custom image to permanently remove it from your account.
