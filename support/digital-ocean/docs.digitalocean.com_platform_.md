# DigitalOcean Platform Overview

Validated on 3 May 2024 • Last edited on 3 Jun 2025

Information about the DigitalOcean platform, like billing details, release notes, product availability by datacenter, support plans, and account details.

## Product Information

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/release-notes.svg)

Release Notes

Release notes track incremental improvements and major releases for the DigitalOcean cloud platform.

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/regional-availability.svg)

Regional Availability

Availability of DigitalOcean products by datacenter and region.

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/product-lifecycle.svg)

Product Lifecycle Stages

Product lifecycle stages define how we stage, release, and retire product functionality. Our product lifecycles stages are private preview, public preview, and general availability (GA).

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/ddos.svg)

DDoS Protection

DigitalOcean DDoS Protection provides free, always-on protection from distributed denial-of-service (DDoS) attacks for applicable DigitalOcean resources.

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/digitalocean.svg)

DigitalOcean IP Ranges (CSV File)

We publish our IP ranges as a CSV (comma-separated value) file, which is updated automatically.

digitalocean.com

## Platform Information

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/accounts.svg)

Accounts

Manage your team membership and your name, sign-in method, and email subscriptions with your personal DigitalOcean account.

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/teams.svg)

DigitalOcean Teams

Teams are how you manage your billing and infrastructure on DigitalOcean. You can work by yourself by remaining the only person on your team or collaborate by adding more people to teams you own.

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/billing.svg)

Billing

Information on billing, including invoices, billing alerts, payment methods, late payments, and taxes.

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/images/icons/digitalocean.svg)

Trust Platform

Security at DigitalOcean

Our six security pillars guide how we secure our products and platform.

Legal Resources

Legal policies and security information, including our terms of service agreement and privacy policy.

Service-level agreements for DigitalOcean products.

## Latest Updates

### 21 November 2025

- We now support Auth0, authentik, JumpCloud, and Keycloak as identity providers for [single sign-on](https://docs.digitalocean.com/platform/teams/how-to/configure-sso/).


### 5 November 2025

- The control panel now logs you out after 12 hours. It notifies you ten minutes and one minute before the timeout so you can log back in. If you have multiple tabs open, logging in again in one tab keeps every tab active.


### 28 August 2025

- [Single sign-on](https://docs.digitalocean.com/platform/teams/how-to/configure-sso/) is now available for teams.


For more information, see [the full release notes](https://docs.digitalocean.com/release-notes/).
