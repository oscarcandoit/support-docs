# How to Create New Teams

Validated on 22 Apr 2025 • Last edited on 30 Sep 2025

When you sign up for DigitalOcean, you are the only member of your default team. After you add a [payment method](https://docs.digitalocean.com/platform/billing/manage-payment-methods/), you can create additional teams.

To create a new team, from the [DigitalOcean Control Panel](https://cloud.digitalocean.com/), click the profile icon in the top right to open the drop-down menu:

![](https://docs.digitalocean.com/screenshots/accounts/profile-icon-menu.d9516ea95d5d74421e062e35c0036b1cc93671e20207676320f4a03c7ccac58f.png)

In the menu that opens, click **Create a Team** to go to the **Create a new team** page. This page has three steps: team info, add payment method, and invite additional team members.

![The create a new team window](https://docs.digitalocean.com/screenshots/teams/create-new.9ff406a3bf1b75512435bc66d9b8c226d2e5a9e8b8a798de0f6ddf34825ccf04.png)

1. On the **Team Info** tab, enter the team name and [contact email](https://docs.digitalocean.com/platform/teams/settings/contact-emails/).

2. On the **Add Payment Method** tab, enter a payment method for the new team. If you already have a payment method associated with your default team, the new team automatically uses the same payment method and this step is skipped.

3. On the **Invite Team Members** tab, you can optionally enter email addresses for people you want to invite to the team, then click **Send Invites**. Alternatively, click **Skip and Go To Team** to remain on a team of one. You can [invite additional people to your team](https://docs.digitalocean.com/platform/teams/how-to/manage-membership/) at any time in the future.

You can also check the box to **Require a secure sign-in method**. If you leave it unchecked, you can [enable secure sign-in](https://docs.digitalocean.com/platform/teams/how-to/require-secure-sign-in/) later.


Once the team is created, you can switch between your teams using profile icon drop-down menu.
