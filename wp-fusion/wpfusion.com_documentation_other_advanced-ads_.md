### [\#](https://wpfusion.com/documentation/other/advanced-ads/\#overview) Overview

WP Fusion integrates with [Advanced Ads](https://wordpress.org/plugins/advanced-ads/) to allow you to control the display of ads on your site based on a logged-in user’s tags.

#### [\#](https://wpfusion.com/documentation/other/advanced-ads/\#setup) Setup

When editing any ad, scroll down to the _Visitor Conditions_ meta box. To add a new WP Fusion condition, select WP Fusion from the _Choose a Condition_ dropdown.

![](https://wpfusion.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/advanced-ads-1024x502.jpg)

There are two options:

- **User is logged in and has at least one of the tags:** The content will only be shown to logged in users who have at least one of the tags specified in the tags select box.
- **User is logged in and has none of the tags:** The content will only be shown to logged in users who do not have _any_ of the tags specified in the tags select box.
