## Search for a Group or Direct Account

Last modified: _2020 April 16_

* * *

## Overview

This interface allows you to search for an individual _Group_ or _Direct Account_.

## Search for a group or direct account

To search for a group or a direct account, perform the following steps:

1. Select your search criteria. You may choose _Email_, _Account_, or _Company_.
2. Enter the search string in the text box.
3. Click _Find_.
