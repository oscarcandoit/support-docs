## Phone Support Incidents

Last modified: _2020 April 7_

* * *

## Overview

The _Phone Support Incidents_ interface displays information for open and closed phone support incidents.

## Open Incidents

The Open Incidents table provides the following information:

- The _Incident_ number.
- The _PIN_ number.
- The _Expiration_ date.
- The _Ticket_ number.

Note:

The _Phone Support Incidents_ feature is available to initiate a support call. To do this, perform the following steps:

1. Click _Call Support_ in the _Action_ column.
2. Enter a phone number for cPanel Support staff to use to call you.
3. Click _Contact Support_.

## Closed Incidents

The _Closed Incidents_ table provides the following information:

- The _Incident_ number.
- The _Pin_ number.
- The _Expiration_ date.
- The _Ticket_ number.
