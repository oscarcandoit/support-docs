## How to Add Custom Sitejet Templates

Last modified: _2025 January 16_

* * *

## Overview

cPanel & WHM [Partners](https://cpanel.net/partners/) can add custom templates to [Sitejet](https://docs.cpanel.net/cpanel/domains/sitejet-builder/) to provide them to their cPanel users.

## Request Sitejet Studio access

To create custom templates, Partners must first gain access to the [Sitejet Studio](https://partner.sitehub.io/) website. Partners can do this either of the following ways:

- Contact their cPanel & WHM sales manager, who will provide access credentials.
- Sign up for their own account on the [Sitejet Studio](https://partner.sitehub.io/) website, then contact their sales manager to enable custom templates.

## Create custom templates

Once a Partner has access to Sitejet Studio, they can log in and add a custom template through one of the following methods:

- [Create a new website-based template.](https://docs.cpanel.net/knowledge-base/sitejet/how-to-add-custom-sitejet-templates/#create-a-new-website-based-template)
- [Add a prebuilt template.](https://docs.cpanel.net/knowledge-base/sitejet/how-to-add-custom-sitejet-templates/#add-a-prebuilt-template)

### Create a new website-based template

To create a new website-based template in Sitejet Studio, navigate to the _Manager_ interface and perform the following steps:

1. Click _\+ Website_. The _Create new website_ menu will appear.
2. In the _Website name_ text box, enter a name for your new website-based template. Click _Add_. The _Templates_ interface will appear.
3. Choose a template from Sitejet’s template library. You may click _Preview_ to evaluate these templates further.
4. When you have found the right template for your business needs, click _Choose_.
5. [Customize your template](https://docs.cpanel.net/knowledge-base/sitejet/how-to-add-custom-sitejet-templates/#customize-a-template).
6. Once you have customized your template, click `Ctrl` and `S` to save, or click the _Save_ icon (![save icon](https://docs.cpanel.net/img/sitejet-saveicon.png)). Then, return to the _Manager_ interface and find your template in the _Websites_ section. It will have the same name you entered in the _Website name_ text box. For example, if you named your template `Custom Template`, look for `Custom Template` here.
7. Open the _Actions_ menu with the three-dot icon (![three-dot menu icon](https://docs.cpanel.net/img/three-dots.png)) and select _Create as template_. The _Create new template_ interface will appear.
8. Enter a new name for your template in the _Website name_ text box, then click _Add_. The _Templates_ interface will appear. Your template will be listed in the _My templates_ section, and will be provided to your cPanel users.

To customize your template from the _My templates_ section, click [_Edit template_](https://docs.cpanel.net/knowledge-base/sitejet/how-to-add-custom-sitejet-templates/#customize-a-template). To return to your template’s interface, click _Manage template_.

### Add a prebuilt template

To add an existing template as a custom template in Sitejet Studio, select _Templates_ in the top navigation bar and perform the following steps:

1. Choose a template from Sitejet’s template library. You may click _Preview_ to evaluate these templates further.
2. When you have found the right template for your business needs, click _Create new_. The _Create New Website_ interface will appear.
3. Enter a new name for your template in the _Website name_ text box, then click _Add_. Your template’s interface will appear. It will have the name you entered in the _Website name_ text box. For example, if you named your template `Custom Template`, your template’s interface will title itself `Custom Template`.
4. To provide this template to your cPanel users without further modification, open the _Actions_ menu by clicking the three-dot icon (![three-dot menu icon](https://docs.cpanel.net/img/three-dots.png)) and select _Create as template_. The _Create new template_ interface will appear.






Note:





To customize the template before providing it to your cPanel users, click [_Edit website_](https://docs.cpanel.net/knowledge-base/sitejet/how-to-add-custom-sitejet-templates/#customize-a-template).

5. Enter a new name for your template in the _Website name_ text box, then click _Add_. The _Templates_ interface will appear. Your template will be listed in the _My templates_ section, and will be provided to your cPanel users.

To customize your template from the _My templates_ section, click [_Edit template_](https://docs.cpanel.net/knowledge-base/sitejet/how-to-add-custom-sitejet-templates/#customize-a-template). To return to your template’s interface, click _Manage template_.

## Customize a template

You can customize your templates with the Sitejet Website Builder (CMS). For more information about how to use this CMS, read Sitejet’s [Website Builder](https://help.sitejet.io/hc/en-us/articles/24275952909463-Overview-Website-Builder-CMS) documentation.
