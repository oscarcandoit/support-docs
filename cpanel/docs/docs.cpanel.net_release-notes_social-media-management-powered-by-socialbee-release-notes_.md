## Social Media Management (Powered by SocialBee) Release Notes

Last modified: _2025 June 3_

* * *

## Social Media Management version 1.6.0-1

_Released June 3, 2025_

### Improved user experience

We improved the user experience in the Social Media Management feature’s landing page, loaders, and generated posts layout.

## Social Media Management version 1.4.0-1

_Released April 8, 2025_

### Improved AI integration

We improved the Social Media Management feature’s AI (Artificial Intelligence) integration to enhance responsiveness and reliability.

## Social Media Management version 1.3.0-1

_Released March 10, 2025_

### Reinitialize on license updates

We added a function to reinitialize the Social Media Management feature when configuration changes, such as buying a cPanel license, affect its installation.

## Social Media Management version 1.1.0-1

_Released February 25, 2025_

### Enabled for version 126 and later

We enabled the Social Media Management feature by default on cPanel & WHM version 126 and later.

## Social Media Management version 1.03-1

_Released February 11, 2025_

### Added to Feature Showcase

We added the Social Media Management feature to WHM’s Feature Showcase.

## Social Media Management version 1.0.1-11

_Released February 3, 2025_

Note:

WebPros International, LLC currently provides this feature in a limited release, with plans to expand its availability in future updates.

### Added Social Media Management feature

We added the Social Media Management feature, powered by [SocialBee](https://socialbee.com/), to the [cPanel interface](https://docs.cpanel.net/cpanel/the-cpanel-interface/the-cpanel-interface/). This feature lets you create, schedule, and reuse content across multiple social media platforms while ensuring consistent engagement. You also get detailed analytics to help you track performance and refine your social media strategy. For more information, read our [_Social Media Management (Powered by SocialBee)_](https://docs.cpanel.net/cpanel/domains/social-media-management/) documentation.
