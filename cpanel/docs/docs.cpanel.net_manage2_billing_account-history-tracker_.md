## Account History Tracker

Last modified: _2019 June 26_

* * *

## Overview

The Account History Tracker interface displays your account’s monthly activity by date.

## Account History table

The _Account History_ table displays your account’s billing, license count, and adjusted license count history for each month. Click _Show Vertical Table_ to display the same information in a vertical format.

## Graphs

The _License History_ and _Billing History_ graphs display your account’s past growth and projected growth based on previous months.

## License History

Click _Show License History_ to display the number of active licenses for your account for previous months, and the projected number of licenses for both past and future months.

## Billing History

Click _Show Billing_ History to display the billing amount for your account for previous months, and the projected billing amount for both past and future months.
