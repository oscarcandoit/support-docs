## Manage Authorized Logo

Last modified: _2019 June 26_

* * *

## Overview

The _Manage Authorized Logo_ interface provides access to your _cPanel Authorized Partner_ logo and a link to your entry in our _Partners_ directory.

## Authorized logo

You can use any of the _cPanel Authorized Partner_ logos on your website. Logos are available with or without your company name, in _Large_, _Medium_, and _Small_ sizes.

To download an image, right-click or Control-click the image and use your browser’s context menu to save the image to your computer.

## Partner Page URL

Use the _Partner Page_ URL link to view your public entry in our Hosting Partner Directory website.
