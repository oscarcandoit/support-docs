## How to Purchase a 360 Monitoring License

Last modified: _2024 April 4_

* * *

## Overview

[360 Monitoring](https://360monitoring.com/) provides users real-time information for their internal and external system monitoring. This includes Server Monitoring for hardware processes, and Site Monitoring to verify fast, consistent performance for your audience.

For more information about how to use 360 Monitoring, visit 360 Monitoring’s official [360 Monitoring documentation](https://docs.360monitoring.com/docs/) page.

## Purchase license from Manage2

Important:

- To buy a 360 Monitoring license through Manage2, you **must** be a cPanel Partner.
- You **must** use the activation link to activate your 360 Monitoring license after purchasing it.

### Log in to Manage2 and select the desired 360 Monitoring package

1. In the [Manage2](https://manage2.cpanel.net/) interface, under the _Licenses_ section, click _Add Licenses_.
2. Select the _Group_ and _Package_ to which to add the 360 Monitoring license.
3. Select the number of _Site Monitors_ and _Server Monitors_ for the license.
4. Click _Add Licenses_.

A success message containing the activation link will appear at the top of the Manage2 interface.

- Follow the link to activate your 360 Monitoring license.
- You will also receive an email message with more details about your license.

For more information, read our [Add License](https://docs.cpanel.net/manage2/licenses/add-license) documentation.
