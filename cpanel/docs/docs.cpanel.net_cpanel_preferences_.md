## Preferences

* * *

#### [Manage Team](https://docs.cpanel.net/cpanel/preferences/manage-team/)

Use this feature to create and manage virtual cPanel accounts.

[Read more](https://docs.cpanel.net/cpanel/preferences/manage-team/)

* * *

#### [Account Preferences](https://docs.cpanel.net/cpanel/preferences/account-preferences/)

Use this interface to change your account settings.

[Read more](https://docs.cpanel.net/cpanel/preferences/account-preferences/)

* * *

#### [Change Language](https://docs.cpanel.net/cpanel/preferences/change-language/)

You can choose to view the cPanel interface in many different languages.

[Read more](https://docs.cpanel.net/cpanel/preferences/change-language/)

* * *

#### [Contact Information](https://docs.cpanel.net/cpanel/preferences/contact-information/)

Use this interface to store contact information for your cPanel account and to set your contact preferences.

[Read more](https://docs.cpanel.net/cpanel/preferences/contact-information/)

* * *

#### [Password & Security](https://docs.cpanel.net/cpanel/preferences/password-and-security/)

This interface allows you to update your account's password.

[Read more](https://docs.cpanel.net/cpanel/preferences/password-and-security/)

* * *

#### [User Manager](https://docs.cpanel.net/cpanel/preferences/user-manager/)

This interface allows you to manage your cPanel account's subaccounts.

[Read more](https://docs.cpanel.net/cpanel/preferences/user-manager/)
