## Address Book

Last modified: _2019 June 26_

* * *

## Overview

The _Address Book_ interface manages all addresses associated with your Manage2 account.

## Add a new address

To add a new address, perform the following steps:

1. Click _Add address_ at the bottom of the interface.
2. Enter a name for the address in the _Address Nickname_ text box. This address nickname identifies the account in your address book.
3. Enter the full name of the contact person in the _Contact_ text box.
4. Enter the contact’s address, city, state or province in the provided text boxes.
5. Select the contact’s country in the _Country_ menu.
6. Enter the contact’s zip or postcode in the _Zip/Postcode_ text box.
7. Click _Save_.

## Update an address

To update an address, perform the following steps:

1. Click _Update_ below the address.
2. Make the changes to the information.
3. Click _Save_.

## Delete an address

To delete an address, click _Delete_ under the address.

Note:

You **cannot** delete an address linked to one or more aspects of your Manage2 account.
